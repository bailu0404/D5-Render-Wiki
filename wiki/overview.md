# D5 Render Overview

D5 Render is a real-time rendering software for architecture and design, built on Unreal Engine and NVIDIA RTX technology with a proprietary high-precision [[global-illumination|GI]] and denoising pipeline. It integrates real-time path tracing, 15+ AI-assisted tools, a rich asset library, and full-workflow scene building capabilities, covering the complete pipeline from concept design to final presentation.

## D5 Full-Workflow Ecosystem (3.0)

D5 Render 3.0 introduces the "Flow State" full-workflow concept, unifying three products:

1. **[[d5-lite]]** — Concept exploration tool, real-time AI visualization inside SketchUp
2. **[[d5-works]]** — AEC professional 3D asset platform
3. **D5 Render 3.0** — High-quality final rendering engine

All managed through the [[d5-launcher|D5 Launcher]] unified entry point.

## Market Position

D5 Render's core advantage lies in combining real-time rendering performance with ease of use, enabling designers to produce high-quality visualization results without deep technical expertise. Version 3.0 further eliminates friction between tools through the full-workflow ecosystem and AI-powered intelligence, allowing creativity to flow seamlessly from concept to final render.

- **User base**: 3M+ professional users from 190+ countries; 50,000+ global enterprise users
- **Market ranking**: G2 #1 rendering software; 80% of world's top 10 architecture firms use D5
- **Data security**: GDPR compliant, ISO 27001 & ISO 27701 certified; no user data used for AI model training
