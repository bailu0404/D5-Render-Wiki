CN-specific account, licensing, and quota details for D5 Render. Covers student edition verification, invoicing, and AI/cloud storage quotas.

## Account Portal

Personal account management: **myspace.d5render.cn**

D5 accounts are unified across D5 Render, D5 Lite, D5 Works, D5 Studio, and D5 Launcher.

## Student Edition (D5学生版)

Free access to D5 Render core features + full asset library.

### Who Qualifies
Students (bachelor/master/doctoral/associate) enrolled in:
- Architecture (建筑学/设计)
- Landscape Architecture (风景园林/设计)
- Urban Planning/Design (城乡规划/设计)
- Environmental Art Design (环境艺术设计)
- Digital Media (数字媒体)
- Product Design (产品设计)
- Film/Animation (影视动画)
- And other spatial design or visual expression programs

### How to Apply
1. Obtain the **教育部学籍在线验证报告** (Ministry of Education Student Status Online Verification Report) in PDF format:
   - Visit 学信网: www.chsi.com.cn
   - Log in (phone/email/WeChat/Alipay)
   - Go to "在线验证报告" section
   - Click "查看" for the 教育部学籍在线验证报告 (complete identity verification if prompted)
   - Click **PDF下载** in the top-right of the report detail page
2. Submit the PDF through the student edition application portal
3. Full guide: d5render.cn/blog-edu-guide/

### Student Benefits (per [[cn-overview]])
- D5 Student Edition: free core + full asset library
- D5 Campus Partners Program (D5校园伙伴计划): ambassador program with training and events
- D5 Cup University Design Competition (D5杯高校设计大赛): annual competition

Individual users (个人身份用户) can request invoices for subscriptions:
- Portal: myspace.d5render.cn → billing section

## AI Credits & Cloud Storage

### Personal Accounts (Community/Professional Edition)

| Resource | Quota |
|----------|-------|
| Cloud storage (total) | 10 GB per account |
| AI Material Generation uses | **50 uses** per quota period (Community Edition) |

Cloud storage covers:
- D5 Studio preset storage (all presets across personal and team spaces)
- All AI task history (image post-processing, material generation)

**To free up space:**
- In D5 Studio: clear presets
- In AI Image Post-Processing panel: clear task history

### Team Edition Accounts

| Resource | Quota |
|----------|-------|
| Cloud storage (total) | **100 GB** per team |

Covers: cloud projects + all members' D5 Studio (personal + team spaces) + all AI tasks.

**Admin management**: Team Admin Portal → Cloud Storage → view current usage → delete items.
Role required: Owner or Administrator.
