D5 Render's Chinese market (大陆及港澳台) presence encompasses the core product, CN-specific enterprise features, an active domestic community, deep academic partnerships, and vocational education engagement. This page documents aspects unique to or particularly emphasized for the Chinese market.

## Company Identity (CN)

D5渲染器 is operated by **江苏维伍喔飞信息科技有限公司** (Jiangsu Viviofly Information Technology Co., Ltd.), headquartered in Nanjing, China. The company's mission: "让更多人可以随心所欲地描绘自己的想象和表达心中美好" — enabling more people to freely depict their imagination and express beauty.

## User Base & Market Position

- **3M+ global registered users** (as of late 2025), with a significant domestic majority
- **30% landscape/garden design** professionals among users
- Enterprise clients: AECOM, 奥雅设计, ASPECT Studios, SWA Shanghai, 山水比德, 上海D+H, UNStudio, and more
- D5 is positioned as an industry-leading real-time rendering tool across architecture, landscape, interior design, and urban planning

## CN-Specific Product Features

### Baidu Netdisk Integration (百度网盘)
Available in [[d5-for-teams|D5 Team Edition]]: direct integration with Baidu Netdisk for cloud-based project file sharing. Enables teams without enterprise NAS infrastructure to share assets through a familiar domestic cloud service.

### D5 Bot (AI 技术支持助手)
An in-app Chinese-language AI chatbot for real-time technical support, launched in D5 2.11 as part of the [[ai-capabilities|AI Design Assistant]]. Provides instant answers to software usage questions 24/7.

### AI Design Assistant (CN Context)
The AI Design Assistant tools are designed with Chinese professional workflows in mind:
- **Flower Garden Generator** (花境生成器): Uses climate and geographic models suited to Chinese planting zones; supports export of plant lists in formats compatible with Chinese landscaping procurement workflows
- **Smart Seedling List** (智能苗木清单): Export includes species name, size classification, count, and optional cost calculation from uploaded price sheets — matching Chinese construction documentation requirements

## Academic & Education Partnerships

### Tsinghua University — Global Plant Database
Collaboration with Tsinghua University School of Architecture (Landscape Architecture Department) and Tsinghua Architectural Design Research Institute on the "D5 Global Landscape Plant Intelligent 3D Database Fundamental Research" project.
- Provides professional botanical taxonomy and growth data for D5's 3900+ plant asset library (1100+ species)
- Research integration enables accurate plant metadata: climate zone, size classification, seasonal variation

### Southeast University (东南大学) Architecture School
Open day event (2025-11-12): students visited D5 offices for hands-on product sessions, introduction to D5 Student Edition and Campus Partners program.

### National Vocational Education Alliance
D5 (江苏维伍喔飞信息科技有限公司) serves as **副理事长单位** (Vice Chairman Unit) of the Cultural & Arts Domain Community of the National Vocational School Teacher Innovation Development Alliance (全国职业学校教师创新发展联盟文化艺术领域共同体), established 2025-09-26. Alliance covers 329 schools and 42 enterprises.

D5's commitment: integrate AI and digital rendering technology into art design vocational education, support "双师型" (dual-qualification teacher) development.

### D5 Campus Partners Program (D5校园伙伴计划)
Student ambassador program:
- Target: architecture, landscape, urban planning, interior design, digital media, product design, animation students (bachelor/master/doctoral/associate)
- Benefits: official event support, exclusive training, branded merchandise, recruitment referrals, priority community access

### D5 Cup University Design Competition (D5杯高校设计大赛)
Ongoing competition for discovering emerging design talent at universities.

### 2026 Campus Recruitment
Global campus recruitment for graduates from Sep 2023 – Aug 2026, covering engineering and design roles. China mainland graduation verified by diploma.

## AI in Chinese Design Industry (2025 Data)

From the D5 + Tencent Research Institute 2025 AI Survey (2490 respondents):

- **85.8%** adoption rate (1825 CN respondents included)
- Domestic AI tools — Tencent Yuanbao (腾讯元宝), Doubao (豆包), DeepSeek — drove ease-of-use improvements that broke adoption barriers
- Previous barriers: "操作复杂" (complex operation) and "海外站点访问困难" (difficulty accessing overseas services) have declined significantly
- Current barrier: cost ("AI需要付费")
- **67.7%** expect AI to be embedded natively in existing design software — driving D5's integration strategy
