D5 Render (CN) offers four subscription tiers sold through the CN official website (d5render.cn). Prices are in RMB and billed through the CN payment system. Always verify current prices at d5render.cn/pricing before quoting.

## Subscription Tiers

### 社区版 Community Edition — 免费 Free

Target: Students, beginners, individual exploration.

Key capabilities included:
- D5 Render + D5 Lite + D5 Works access
- Image rendering up to 16K
- Video output up to 4K
- 1 virtual tour project
- 2,100+ official assets
- AI Design Assistant: limited uses (50 times for most features)
- 5 D5 Studio personal presets
- Cloud storage: limited

**Best for**: Learning the software, small personal projects, evaluating before purchase.

---

### 专业版 Professional Edition — ¥200/月 或 ¥1920/年

Target: Practising designers, freelancers, individual studios.

| Billing | Price | Effective Monthly Rate |
|---------|-------|----------------------|
| 按月订阅 | ¥200/月 | ¥200/月 |
| 按年订阅 | ¥1920/年 | ¥160/月（节省20%）|

Everything in Community, plus:
- Unlimited virtual tour projects
- Unlimited AI Design Assistant usage
- 16,000+ official assets
- 10 GB cloud storage (D5 Studio)
- Unlimited personal presets
- Full virtual tour platform features (password protection, hotspots, floor plan navigation, sharing controls)
- Video output up to 4K
- Invoicing (发票) available via D5 User Center

**Best for**: Professional rendering work, client presentations, individuals who need the full asset library and unlimited AI usage.

---

### 团队版 Team Edition — 联系商务 Contact Sales

Target: Design firms, enterprise teams, multi-department organizations.

Everything in Professional, plus:
- Video output up to **8K**
- Unlimited AI Design Assistant
- 100 GB cloud storage
- Multi-user real-time project editing
- Team asset library (shared company models/materials)
- Team presets
- Admin dashboard (role-based permissions, analytics)
- Baidu Netdisk (百度网盘) integration
- Rendering farm (distributed rendering across team workstations)
- Enterprise project library (LAN deployment, data security)
- SSO enterprise login

**Best for**: Firms with multiple designers, projects requiring collaboration, enterprise data security requirements.

---

### 学生版 Student Edition — 免费 Free

Target: Enrolled university students in architecture, landscape, interior design, animation, and related programs.

Access level: equivalent to Professional Edition core features.

Verification requirement: 教育部学籍在线验证报告 (Ministry of Education Student Status Online Verification Report, PDF format).

**Best for**: University students who qualify; provides full professional tools at no cost during study.

See [[cn-account]] for the verification process.

## Purchasing & Billing

- **Professional**: Purchased directly at d5render.cn/pricing or d5render.cn/d5-pro/
- **Team**: Contact sales at d5render.cn/team/ or via d5render.cn/price-form
- **Student**: Apply via self-service portal at d5render.cn/edu/
- **Subscription rules**: Full details at d5render.cn/subscription-rules/
- **Invoicing**: After purchase, request via D5 User Center → Invoice Service (cn.myspace.d5render.com/bill)

## Upgrade Path

- Community → Professional: purchase directly online
- Professional → Team: contact sales (existing Pro subscription credits may apply)
- Student → Professional: once student verification expires, purchase Professional

## Annual vs Monthly (Professional)

Annual subscription (¥1920/年) saves ¥480 compared to 12 months of monthly billing (¥2400/年). Recommended for users who plan to use D5 continuously throughout the year.

## CN vs International Pricing

The CN pricing page (d5render.cn/pricing) and the international pricing page (d5render.com/pricing) are separate. CN pricing is in RMB; international pricing is in USD. Feature tiers are structurally identical, though exact prices and payment methods differ. CN users should always use the CN portal.
