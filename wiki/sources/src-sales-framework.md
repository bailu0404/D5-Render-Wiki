# D5 Render Sales Argument Framework

Internal battle card framework organizing D5's competitive advantages into three tiers: USP (unique to D5), Leading (D5 is stronger), and Parity (D5 matches competitors).

> **Source**: Internal Battle Cards + Engagement & Message Matrix, compiled 2026-03-11. INTERNAL USE ONLY.

## Tier 1: USP — Unique to D5

### Engine Architecture (must be stated accurately)

- **D5 Render** = Built on UE5, deeply customized for architectural workflows, WYSIWYG consistency is the core value
- **D5 Lite** = Powered by D5's proprietary engine (D5 Engine), built from scratch for spatial design
- **Roadmap** = D5 Engine will be integrated into D5 Render, gradually replacing the UE5 foundation

### All-in-One AI Workflow Ecosystem

D5 Lite + D5 Render + D5 Works unified: concept-to-production-to-assets in one account, one workflow. No competitor offers this integrated product line.

### 15+ Integrated AI Tools

Including AI Scene Match, AI Smart Planting, AI PBR Material Snap, AI Image to 3D, AI Style Transfer, AI Enhancer, AI Make Seamless. Lumion/Enscape/Twinmotion/V-Ray lack these or only have point-solution AI like denoising.

### Multi-user Real-time Collaborative Editing (VCP)

Multiple users editing the same render scene simultaneously, with shared asset libraries, project comments, permission management, and data dashboards. No competitor supports real-time multi-user editing of the same scene.

### XR Tour (3D Gaussian Splatting)

Render scenes converted to 3D spaces accessible from any browser without software installation. No competitor has this feature.

### Enterprise SSO + Complete Teams Solution

SSO single sign-on, app integrations, 100GB cloud storage, Showreel platform. Lumion/Enscape have enterprise licensing but lack comparable collaboration platform depth.

## Tier 2: Leading — Everyone Has It, but D5 Is Stronger

| Dimension | D5 Advantage |
|-----------|-------------|
| Rendering quality | Real-time path tracing vs. rasterization (Lumion/Enscape); 11x faster image output than Lumion; 18x faster than V-Ray with equal or better quality |
| Asset library count | 14,000+ built-in (+39,200+ on D5 Works) vs. Lumion ~9,100, Enscape ~1,400 |
| Learning curve | 30 minutes to onboard vs. V-Ray's steep professional curve |
| Modeling software support | 8 tools (SketchUp/Rhino/Revit/3ds Max/Blender/ArchiCAD/Cinema 4D/Vectorworks) vs. Enscape 4-5 |
| Large scene handling | Tiered acceleration + Texture Streaming, validated by BIG/Nihon Sekkei |
| Pricing | D5 Pro $360/year vs. Lumion Pro $1,299/year, Enscape Floating $958.83/year |

## Tier 3: Parity — Competitors Do Well, D5 Also Has

- **VR support**: Twinmotion strongest (UE-based); D5 also supports SteamVR + spatial/panorama tours
- **Revit integration**: Enscape's native plugin tighter for Revit-only; D5 LiveSync supports 8 tools including Revit, plus multi-user collaboration
- **Floating license**: Enscape/Lumion have it; D5 does not — acknowledged shortcoming, offset by lower Teams pricing
- **Quick animation**: Lumion good for basic fly-through; D5 also has complete animation tools with 3.0 speed boost (1-min animation from 6 days → 30 minutes)
- **Revit bundling**: Twinmotion bundled free with Revit; D5 Community edition free with more complete features
