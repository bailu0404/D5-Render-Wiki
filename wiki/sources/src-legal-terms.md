# D5 Legal Documents — Terms of Service, Privacy Policy

Summary of D5's three main legal documents: D5 Render Terms of Service, General Terms of Service, and Privacy Policy. These govern use of all D5 products and services.

> **Source**: Official legal pages at d5render.com, clipped 2026-04-17.

## Entity Structure

- **Dimension 5 PTE. LTD.** (Singapore) — serves users outside the United States; responsible for core product infrastructure, unified account systems, product delivery, technical operations, security
- **Dimension 5 LLC** (Delaware, USA) — serves users in the United States; responsible for sales, billing, payment collection, customer support, account management

## D5 Render Terms of Service — Key Points

### Ownership and License

- Users retain all rights and ownership of their Content
- D5 does **not** use user Content as training data without express written consent
- D5 grants itself a limited license to process, store, modify, and display Content solely for service delivery
- AI feature vendors/partners process Content only as necessary and do not use it for model training

### Scene Capture & AI Processing

- D5 Render includes automated scene capture for AI features (e.g., Scene Atmosphere Optimization, AI Enhancer)
- Captured scene data is used solely for generating reference images and rendering parameters
- D5 will not use scene data for foundational model training without separate written consent

### Material Library License

- **Personal Use** materials: non-commercial only; cannot be used in paid/client work
- **Project Use** materials: can be used in commercial projects, client deliverables, promotional materials
- Mixing Personal Use and Project Use materials in a single project makes the entire project non-commercial
- No ownership transfer — D5 or its licensors retain all rights

### AI Features

- Users own both Input and Output
- D5 will not publicly disclose, publish, or use Output for marketing/training without express consent
- Output may not be unique due to AI nature
- Users are responsible for evaluating AI Output accuracy and appropriateness

### Third-Party Components

- Panoramic viewing uses krpano (licensed from krpano GmbH)
- Users may not self-host krpano viewer files, remove logos, or modify/decompile krpano components

## General Terms of Service — Key Points

### Account Types

- **Named User License** (Enterprise/Teams): personal, non-transferable, not concurrent. Cannot be shared across individuals.
- **Personal Account** (Individual): Single Individual License. One natural person per account. Multi-device allowed for same user.

### Payment & Cancellation

- Subscriptions auto-renew; cancel before next renewal
- Payments non-refundable for current period (except where required by law)
- EEA consumers: 14-day withdrawal right with prorated refund
- Price increases require 14 days' notice, effective at next renewal

### Dispute Resolution

- **US users**: Binding arbitration via AAA, New York governing law
- **Non-US users**: Binding arbitration via SIAC, Singapore governing law
- Class action waiver applies
- 60-day pre-arbitration dispute resolution period

### Content Restrictions

- No infringement of IP rights
- No CSAM or content depicting minors in sexual contexts
- No misrepresentation of AI-generated content (tampering with AI labels/metadata is prohibited)
- No use for gambling, nuclear facilities, air traffic control, or military live combat

## Privacy Policy — Key Points

### Data Collection

- **Provided by user**: Registration info, profile data, education verification, team management data, forum posts, support requests, AI feature inputs
- **Automatically collected**: Device info, usage data, cookies, search queries, order/payment info
- **From third parties**: SSO providers (Google/Microsoft), social platforms, CRM systems

### AI Data Protection

- D5 does **not** use AI interaction content (prompts, questions, outputs) to train general-purpose AI models by default
- If future training use is planned, D5 will obtain prior consent and provide opt-out mechanisms
- AI interaction content may be used for product safety, security, debugging, or quality assurance (proportionate)

### Data Sharing

- Resellers: limited info for license administration, renewal, support
- Advertising partners: hashed email addresses for campaign measurement (opt-out available)
- Between Dimension 5 entities: for service delivery, billing, support, security
- Legal reasons: law enforcement, fraud prevention, safety

### Data Retention

- Personal data deleted within 1 month of account cancellation
- Longer retention for invoicing, tax, fraud prevention, legal compliance as permitted

### User Rights

- Access, portability, correction, deletion, objection, consent withdrawal
- California residents: CCPA/CPRA rights including opt-out of "sale"/"sharing"
- EEA residents: GDPR rights with Standard Contractual Clauses for data transfers

### Security

- ISO 27001 & ISO 27701 certified
- GDPR compliant
- Encryption and authentication tools for data protection
