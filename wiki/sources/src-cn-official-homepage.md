## Key Takeaways

### Product Matrix (三件套 / Three-Product Ecosystem)

| Product | Role | Key Pitch |
|---------|------|-----------|
| **D5 Lite** | AI real-time rendering plugin | Embedded in modeling software; from sketch to detailed design without switching apps |
| **D5 Render** | Professional real-time rendering software | Handles large scenes; AI accelerates material editing, scene building, atmosphere; outputs animation and virtual tours |
| **D5 Works** | 3D asset platform | Global high-quality 3D models and materials; seamless integration into D5 workflow |

### Market Scale (as of 2026)
- **Global users**: 3,000,000+
- **Global enterprise clients**: 50,000+
- **G2 Ranking**: #1 in architectural rendering software category

### Industry Solutions

**建筑设计 (Architecture)**
Covers large-scale projects; team collaboration and client communication platform. Key client: BIG (Bjarke Ingels Group).

**景观设计 (Landscape Design)**
PCG landscape tools + AI Design Assistant. Supports natural landscapes at any scale. Replaces hard software-switching with real-time "live-link" workflow.

**室内设计 (Interior Design)**
Photorealistic real-time rendering for design decisions. Client presentation via animation and virtual tours.

**D5 Lite integration** (implied): architecture, interior, and garden design workflows embedded directly in SketchUp, Rhino, 3ds Max, Revit, ArchiCAD, C4D, Blender, Vectorworks.
