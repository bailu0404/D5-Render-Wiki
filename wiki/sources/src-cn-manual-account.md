## Account Registration & Login

D5 accounts are shared across all D5 products (D5 Render, D5 Lite, D5 Works, D5 Studio, D5 Launcher). Registration and login via d5render.cn.

**Error — device limit**: If a login prompt shows "该账号登录设备超出限制，请解绑设备后再尝试" (this account has exceeded device login limit), unbind a device from the account portal at myspace.d5render.cn before retrying.

*Source: raw/cn/manual/account.md, raw/cn/manual/error-message -- c652.md*

## Student Edition (D5学生版)

D5 Student Edition offers free access to core features and the full asset library for qualified students.

### Eligibility
- Students in related majors: architecture, landscape architecture, urban planning/design, environmental art design, digital media, product design, film/animation, and related programs
- Bachelor, Master, Doctoral, and Associate (高职/专科) levels are all eligible

### Verification Process
Students must provide an **教育部学籍在线验证报告** (Ministry of Education Student Status Online Verification Report) in PDF format.

**How to obtain the report:**
1. Visit 学信网 (www.chsi.com.cn)
2. Log in with phone/email + password, or scan with WeChat/Alipay
3. Navigate to "在线验证报告" section
4. Click "查看" on the 教育部学籍在线验证报告 (identity verification may be required)
5. On the report detail page, click the **PDF下载** icon in the top right corner to save locally

**Reference**: Detailed steps and common issues at d5render.cn/blog-edu-guide/

## Invoicing (发票)

Individual identity users (个人身份用户) can request an invoice (发票凭证) for their subscription purchases via the personal account portal: myspace.d5render.cn.

## AI Credits & Cloud Storage Quotas

### Personal Users (社区版/专业版)
- **10 GB** cloud storage per account
- Storage covers: all D5 Studio preset saves + all AI task history
- Community edition users: AI Material Generation is counted within **50 uses** per quota period
- To free up space: clear presets in D5 Studio, or clear AI image post-processing task history in the AI panel

### Team Edition Users
- **100 GB** cloud storage per team
- Covers: cloud projects + all members' personal/team D5 Studio spaces + all AI tasks
- Storage management:
  - From client: clear Studio presets, clear AI image history
  - From Team Admin Portal: Owner/Admin roles can view storage usage and delete items at Team Admin → Cloud Storage
