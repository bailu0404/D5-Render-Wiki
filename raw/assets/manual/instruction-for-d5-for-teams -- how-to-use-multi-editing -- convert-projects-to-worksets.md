D5 Render will automatically classify all models in the scene file into the "Default Workset". You can create custom worksets based on your project requirements and rename them, such as "Landscape", "Architecture", "Interior", etc.

![](https://docs.d5render.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3611830798-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F-MjbN1iGmN_HBnj_fyU9%252Fuploads%252F3Hz0AmpujOK1ss4iBi97%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D549c15a2-0cef-42c5-ab9e-75ca726563d7&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=476e722c&sv=2)![](https://docs.d5render.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3611830798-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F-MjbN1iGmN_HBnj_fyU9%252Fuploads%252FawlG3kNPGC7EJfKqcvqs%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D493d63ae-90c7-47e1-9d94-01a8f0306bbb&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=27481a1a&sv=2)![](https://docs.d5render.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3611830798-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F-MjbN1iGmN_HBnj_fyU9%252Fuploads%252FuVhPy5423TRrqsCUMjn8%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D965c0e56-2425-4f66-90c1-7f039778b815&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=8a464569&sv=2)

Once you exit the central file, other team members can enter different worksets simultaneously.

![](https://docs.d5render.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3611830798-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F-MjbN1iGmN_HBnj_fyU9%252Fuploads%252FL0XHsK7MISgPaDZzb9GU%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D4e32a623-7695-4ed3-a1c2-94a9762f97bd&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=96237939&sv=2)

Open the central file to check and edit assets in all worksets, or make adjustments in time.
