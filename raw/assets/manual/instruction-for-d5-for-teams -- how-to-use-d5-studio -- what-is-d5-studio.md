# What is D5 Studio？

> D5 Studio is a cloud workspace created by D5 Render, in Studio you can sink your personal knowledge-based assets and share the accumulated experience of your personal design experience to the team. D5 Studio is designed to provide better productivity & creativity enhancement for creative projects.

Data in Studio is stored in the cloud associated with your D5 account. Even if you ****change devices**** or ****work off-site****, you'll have access to all of your and your team's Studio resources.
