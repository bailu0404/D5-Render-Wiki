- Terrain materials

You can edit materials on different areas of the terrain.

![](https://docs.d5render.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Flh7-rt.googleusercontent.com%2Fdocsz%2FAD_4nXdTj3s9Yn6QRb1RfyM-DBxFTONoMkt7Dj8WNB0YEQxcoJqhCXeGuN7JHrOgCUjf8MyWb_qD8qidLYQPhBhxqUqh_JUc3zTOiPv68m_PsxdUNi3H3okFcVLWPwurpSQjASKFRi7N%3Fkey%3DFj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=db3accdc&sv=2)

The ground section manages the textures on the ground surface. We can layer up to six different textures to enrich the ground's appearance.

The slope section controls the material of the middle segment.

The peak texture determines the material at the top of a mountain.

Every texture supports advanced settings similar to other materials.

First off, hover over the "Add Scatter" button. You can create a scatter area by selecting the material or model.

![](https://docs.d5render.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Flh7-rt.googleusercontent.com%2Fdocsz%2FAD_4nXeppbWZbTKpQmMBLbKp-EeDsYMiStsnp8UrLd2fCuCv1btQ00wlrW33F5OMWswZjchTdLYlfMVTCZZ1mS0rc4Cny5BI1dvUiVy3Hy3VhUpDMwM21FjHD5pooL8xNRnCGAYOHYdO%3Fkey%3DFj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=2d4648b8&sv=2)
