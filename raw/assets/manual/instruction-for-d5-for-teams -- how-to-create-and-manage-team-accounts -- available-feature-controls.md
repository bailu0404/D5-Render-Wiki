# Available Feature Controls

> ℹ️ > This feature requires a whitelist for activation and is intended for enterprise clients. The ****team owner**** or ****super administrator**** can determine which specific functions or behaviors are accessible to team members within the D5 client as necessary.

> ****Note:****
>
> To activate this feature, please get in touch with the relevant sales representative or customer success manager ([[email protected]](https://docs.d5render.com/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection)), and provide the team owner's account. Only after activation shall this section appear within MySpace.

Entrance: The team owner or super administrator with this feature activated can log in to [D5 MySpace](https://myspace.d5render.cn/) and navigate to `Settings > Workspace` to access the ****Available Feature Controls**** section.

#### ****AI Features****

Once this switch is turned off, team members will no longer have access to AI features in D5 Render. If a member attempts to use any AI-related feature in the D5 client, a prompt will notify them that the team has disabled this feature.

![](https://docs.d5render.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3611830798-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F-MjbN1iGmN_HBnj_fyU9%252Fuploads%252FGiZTMhgktzp3vPWaMrMA%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D68805636-051e-4a9f-8be0-fa653b10686a&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=697f155e&sv=2)![](https://docs.d5render.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3611830798-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F-MjbN1iGmN_HBnj_fyU9%252Fuploads%252FsfWRVEOvIGLh1yLjNmXD%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Debdae61e-3610-4274-a672-261ed35b9c27&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=b5aa05b2&sv=2)

Prompt

The AI functions involved in this control are as follows:

- Ultra HD Texture
- Make Seamless
- AI Material Snap
- AI-generated texture maps
- D5 SR Image Rendering beta
- AI Post-Processing
- AI Atmosphere Match
- AI Model Generation

#### ****Client Update****

When disabled, each member's D5 client remains on its current version and no longer receives update notifications.
