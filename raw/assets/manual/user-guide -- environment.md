# Sky and Weather

This section explains the meaning of each parameter in the Environment panel in detail. The Environment panel is divided into two main parts that control the atmosphere of the scene together.

## 🌤️ Sky Light

[How to adjust parameters of Sky Light?](https://docs.d5render.com/user-guide/environment/sky)

👆Control the natural light environment, i.e. the sun and the sky. HDRI lighting can also be used to customize the background and environment light.

## ❄️ Weather

[How to adjust the weather in D5?](https://docs.d5render.com/user-guide/environment/weather)

👆Adjusting clouds, fog and wind parameters to bring realism to the scene.
