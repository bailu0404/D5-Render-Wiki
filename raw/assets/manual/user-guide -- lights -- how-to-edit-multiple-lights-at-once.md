# How to edit multiple lights at once?

After grouping lights of the same type (Point Light/Spotlight/Strip Light/Rect Light/Stage Light/Projector (projection light)), the parameters of the lights will be automatically associated. When you select a group of lights in the scene or list, the right sidebar will automatically display the corresponding parameters of that type of light for uniform adjustment.
