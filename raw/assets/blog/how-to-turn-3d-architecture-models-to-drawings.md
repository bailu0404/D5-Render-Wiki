# How to turn 3d architecture models to drawings in seconds

### ****What It Does****

After designers build their models and connect them to D5 Render, they can add supplementary elements like plants or vehicles—or simply render the scene as-is. Once ****AI Style Transfer**** is activated, D5 Render automatically transforms the rendered image into a hand-drawn style.

This feature is particularly useful when architecture drawings are required. Instead of manually sketching architectural concepts each time a modification is made, designers can rely on AI to generate stylized visuals instantly.

You can choose to ****fine-tune your models and renders to an ideal condition**** before converting them into drawings. Alternatively, you can ****edit your models first**** and generate rough hand-drawn-style renderings at each stage, then create polished 3D visualizations once the model is finalized.

In short, it streamlines the workflow:  
****Basic model → Architecture drawing → Refined model → Realistic render****
