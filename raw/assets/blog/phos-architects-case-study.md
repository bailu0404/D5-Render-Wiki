# Phos Architects Case Study: D5 Render Workflow Secrets

![phos architects ](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/673c2da8e1c1500e0c7324b0_673c28afcbb378bcc0a9abdd_PHOS%25205%25201.png)

As an award-winning boutique firm in hotel and restaurant design, [PHOS Architects](https://phos-architects.com/) approaches each project as a story told through textures, shapes, and sensations. They're not just building structures; they're crafting atmospheres—spaces that feel as though they could breathe. In their journey to capture these nuances, they found a powerful partner in **D5 Render**, a tool that transforms their sketches into immersive, lifelike experiences.

### ‍****Key Takeaways:****

- ****Real-Time Experimentation:**** D5 Render allows PHOS Architects to test lighting, materials, and textures live, providing instant feedback and streamlining design iterations.
- ****Enhanced Efficiency and Reduced Energy Costs:**** D5 halves the time PHOS Architects used to spend on renders and reduces the need for overnight PC use.
- ****Client Collaboration in Real-Time:**** D5 Render's live capabilities enable clients to visualize changes on the spot, making the design process a shared experience.
- ****Cutting Down on Post-Production:**** With a comprehensive asset library and built-in material features, D5 minimizes the need for external editing, saving time and preserving the integrity of the design.

For PHOS, the mission is clear: take on fewer projects but pour their all into each one with meticulous care. D5 Render matches their vision, enabling rapid experimentation and refinement with remarkable precision.

## Breaking Free from Past Constraints

> **"D5 lets us produce many more images, giving the client a rounded view of the project. Now, instead of 10 images, we can show them 50, offering far more detail in much less time."**

Previously, PHOS Architects used software like V-Ray and Lumion but found themselves limited by sluggish render times and high energy costs. Switching to D5 Render was a breath of fresh air.

“Before, we'd leave our computers running overnight just to get a single high-quality render. With D5, we can achieve those results in half the time and without keeping PCs on all night,” PHOS co-founder Stefanos shared, describing how D5's speed and efficiency have transformed their process. Now, projects that once took two or three days to prepare for rendering can be completed in just a day, leaving more time for creativity and less spent on waiting.

## Showcasing the Heart of Every Project

> **"D5 helps us capture the vibe, not just the visuals. It's one thing to show a well-lit image, but it's another to convey how a space actually feels."**

PHOS Architects begins each project with an in-depth conversation, focusing not just on design needs but on the mood and soul their clients envision. The team sketches by hand, refines in [**Rhino**](https://www.d5render.com/workflow/rhino?utm_source=d5blog&utm_medium=seo&utm_campaign=phos-architects), and then transitions to D5 Render, where the magic happens. Here, they play with lighting, materials, and textures in real time, building a virtual world that echoes the reality they aim to create.

![phos architects ](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/673c2da7e1c1500e0c732458_673c2ad4c02f457abead175f_PHOS%2520d5%2520vs%2520built.png)

One project that highlights this was a pair of boutique spas in Athens—a Japanese and a Russian spa—each with its own unique ambiance. Using D5, PHOS fine-tuned lighting and materials to capture the calming, immersive atmospheres essential to these spaces. "We can see our ideas evolve live, so by the time it reaches the client, it's already something we're proud of," Stefanos explained. D5 Render's tools enable PHOS to go beyond visuals, conveying the true spirit of every space they create.

## Real-Time Client Collaboration: Designing Together

D5 Render has also transformed client presentations, making it possible for clients to be part of the design process in real time. During meetings, clients can instantly see adjustments to materials or colors, allowing them to collaborate in ways that weren't possible before.

![phos architects ](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/673c2da8e1c1500e0c73248b_673c2b19297d0803abd90494_PHOS%25203%25201.png)

“If a client wants to try a different wood texture, we can change it in seconds, right there in the meeting,” he explained. “It's not just impressive—it's effective. The client can see the project evolve and give feedback instantly, making them feel truly invested in the design.”

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/68301f78150a3525a3a576ca_aa.png)

## Simplifying Post-Production and Reducing Energy Costs

D5 Render's robust library of [built-in assets](https://www.d5render.com/assets?utm_source=d5blog&utm_medium=seo&utm_campaign=phos-architects) has dramatically reduced the time PHOS Architects spends on post-production. With a wide range of materials, plants, and accessories ready to go, the team no longer needs to rely heavily on Photoshop, as they did in the past. Now, everything from plants to intricate textures can be fine-tuned directly in D5, saving time and allowing them to present images that are polished straight out of the software.
