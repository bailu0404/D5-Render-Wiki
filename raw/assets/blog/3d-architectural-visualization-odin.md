## ****1. A Bold Move from a Veteran Studio****

Nestled between Vietnam and France, Odin Architect is a boutique design and visualization studio that thrives on both precision and passion. From the very first sketch to the final render, their work reflects a delicate balance of artistry and efficiency—brought to life by a small yet globally connected team. Despite a compact team of under 10 full-time members, their reputation spans continents—built on aesthetic clarity, efficient delivery, and meticulous detail. For over a decade, they operated with a refined workflow centered on Lumion rendering. But as client expectations and project scales evolved, so did their ambition.

> **"We had over 10 years of experience using Lumion, but we were ready for more."**

—Linh Nguyen Hoang, Project Manager at Odin Architect

## ****2.**** The Legacy Setup: A Mature 3D Architectural Visualization Workflow

Before discovering D5, Odin Architect worked within a tried-and-true setup: SketchUp and 3ds Max for modeling, then Lumion for rendering. Over time, they had built a robust custom asset library—carefully curated through real client work and external purchases. This Lumion rendering system offered predictability and efficiency, but it wasn’t without its friction.

The team often found themselves walking a tightrope. To keep Lumion responsive, they had to meticulously optimize every file. Preview renders rarely reflected the final quality, forcing time-consuming test cycles and multiple adjustments.

"We had to constantly optimize files to avoid overload… which ultimately limited the quality of the final output."
