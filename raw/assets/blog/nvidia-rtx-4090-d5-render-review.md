The ultimate GeForce GPU, RTX 4090 is finally here! The long-awaited graphics card is said to bring an enormous leap in performance, efficiency, AI-powered graphics. It's powered by the NVIDIA Ada Lovelace architecture, featuring ****16,384 CUDA cores****, running ****24 GB**** of G6X video memory on a 384-bit bus. With more extensive raytracing features, upgraded Tensor cores and DLSS 3.0, 4090 should be capable of running real-time rendering software [D5 Render](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nvidia-rtx-4090-d5-render-review) to provide the fastest and smoothest possible real-time rendering experience at an amazing frame rate.Will it show it show generational boost to double the performance compared to the preceding RTX 30 Series as rumored? Is it worthwhile to buy it for productive work? Find out in this article. Tests will be done on 4K monitor with architecture, interior and gaming render scenes.

## ****D5 Benchmark****

We will be using [D5 Render Benchmark Tool](https://usa.asset.d5cdn.com/benchmark/D5Benchmark_en.exe). By running this tool, which automatically starts testing on the same scene, you'll see how well your computer performs when it comes to ****real-time operating**** and ****rendering output**** in [D5 Render](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nvidia-rtx-4090-d5-render-review). We'll compare the performance of RTX4090 to 3090 on this same D5 scene with 6 million faces, 12 light sources and 299 maps.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/688c5bbbe8a1bf612b1231ad_634fda076b017760c8ce425f_152.png)

It took 3090 3 minutes 44 seconds to finish the rendering test while 4090 made it in ****1 minute 45 seconds****.For a 2k resolution image, 3090 renders in 33 seconds, and 4090 ****19**** seconds.For a 5-second-long animation, 3090 renders in 3 minutes 11 seconds, and 4090 ****1 minute 26 seconds****.All these test results can be found [here](https://www.d5render.com/result). As you can see, 3060 here still shows great capacity with a total time of 3 minute 23 second to render an animation 5 seconds long.

### ****Real-time rendering****

We've tested several features and commands inside [D5 Render](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nvidia-rtx-4090-d5-render-review) on the scenes, and it turned out that the beastly 4090 is extremely competitive in working with D5. By using D5's built-in path/scatter/brush tools to quickly place multiple car/people/vegetation assets, which is normally resource-intensive, we can still expect a smooth and efficient rendering experience with a relatively high frame rate.

## **Conclusion**

Comparing the raw performance of the star 4090 with 3090, we saw a 100% uplift in both real-time FPS and rendering speed. The former proves to be a stronger performer judging from the synthetic benchmarks and real-world performance.

It's safe to conclude now that RTX4090 is a beast of card for ****3d rendering professionals****. It can handle production-level archviz rendering ****twice as fast as 3090**** while maintaining an FPS high enough to guarantee a smooth real-time rendering experience.

Its futuristic features should work especially well with [D5](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nvidia-rtx-4090-d5-render-review) in shortening render time drastically, handling extremely huge/complex scenes and offering an incredibly smooth real-time experience.

**Is 4090 worth the money?** Whether you have an answer right now or not, what we can advise is that, apart from a hardware upgrade, switching to a better, easier, more affordable software like [D5](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=nvidia-rtx-4090-d5-render-review) will also be a boost to your efficiency. Despite its amazing performance with 4090, this software is not limited to high-end cards only, and runs on ****GeForce GTX 1060 6GB**** and above. (More on [D5 Render minimum requirements](https://www.d5render.com/post/system-requirements-for-d5-render)) It's also coming to a wider range of graphics cards in the near future, bringing next-generation rendering workflow to more architects, designers, and 3d artists.
