### ****D5 AI: Make Creation Easier & More Efficient****

What's stealing the show in the new year? Without a doubt, it's artificial intelligence! Ideas, materials, styles, just one click away. D5 has gone live in April 2023 with [D5 Hi](http://d5next.ai/) to help you generate images and get inspiration. Furthermore, the groundbreaking AI features in [D5 2.6](https://www.d5render.com/posts/d5-render-updates) play a pivotal role in propelling your rendering productivity to new heights, making visualization effortlessly for you.

- ****AI-Generated Material Texture Maps:****  Algorithms automatically generates normal, roughness and height channel maps according to the base color map of the material.
