## ****Where AI Accelerates — From Hours to Minutes****

### ****Smart Planting (AI Agent)****

With ****AI Agent → SmartPlanting****, designers simply describe the context—region, color palette, climate, season—and D5 generates:

- a ****curated plant list**** (habit, seasonal interest, light/water needs)
- a ****designed planting layout**** with intentional grouping patterns
- a one-click ****Auto Scatter**** placement ready for refinement

You can remove species, resize individual plants, or fine-tune density at any time.  
For large sites, what used to take hours becomes minutes—with full artistic control preserved.

### ****AI Atmosphere Match****

A single click aligns atmosphere with your reference image. D5 adjusts sun angle, haze, fog, and environment to that mood in seconds—perfect when the composition is right but the feeling isn’t. Iterate until the atmosphere feels true.

### ****Scatter, Advanced Brush, and Manual Hero Placement****

Use **Scatter** for meadows, slopes, and hillsides; **Advanced Brush** to paint shrubs with a modifiable brush paths. For key vistas, manually place hero trees that frame architecture without hiding crucial design moves—because composition is human.

### ****Terrain You Can Sculpt****

[D5’s terrain](https://www.d5render.com/posts/architectural-landscape-terrain-rendering/https://www.d5render.com/posts/what-is-real-time-path-tracing-in-d5-render-2-10?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=ai-landscaping-design) system lets you shape context directly inside the renderer:

- Use **Upward / Downward / Smooth / Flatten** to model mountains, berms, terraces, and lakes.
- Assign altitude-based materials (forest ground at peaks, grass on slopes) and blend via altitude/range controls.
- Paint beach or lakebed details, add a water plane, and fine-tune color, flow, and scale for natural movement.

## ****Real-Time Rendering That Feels Like Painting****

****Real-time Path Tracing**** is now the default visual mode in D5. With real-time ray tracing and global illumination, you read tone and depth—not just geometry. Nudge the sun, dial fog, or swap HDRIs and watch the scene breathe.

Add volumetric fog, bloom, motion blur, and ambient occlusion for emotional realism. Switch to ****Walk Mode****, adjust height and speed, and move through the model as if exploring the site.

## ****AI Post-Processing That Respects Your Eye****

![ai landscaping design](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/6920510cdbc7d2e53ac6c388_5eecdaf48460cde52795ef727582ff5de8e2b38dee57067375b8339e1c4c2483f728f271dbcdaa6639e8703ac5556d0d46f61eaa23e2a7836568d8594e3024b09c91ad7f0c6d3d3ba8b697109b8701d239bd5f097366bab69c7c5af15e691c51.png)

Once your render feels right, ****AI Post-Processing**** refines it—never overrides.

![ai landscaping design](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/6923c26f32407b186400d830_Group997-ezgif.com-apng-to-avif-converter.avif)

- ‍****AI Inpainting**** helps smooth out thin or uneven vegetation by adding gentle density and variation where it’s needed. It respects the scene’s scale and context, making both close-up plant beds and wide aerial greens feel more cohesive. It’s a simple way to fine-tune landscape realism without stepping outside your workflow.****‍****
- ****AI Enhancer**** lifts figures or small assets that read too digital—perfect for the one person standing in the sun.
- The same applies to ****Isometric or Parallel Projection**** aerials: render, then inpaint green zones for clarity and depth.

These are accelerators, not autopilots—you decide what changes, and by how much.

## ****From Image to Insight: Scenes, Animations, Communication****

Define three to four key scenes that explain structure and circulation clearly. Then layer in atmosphere and camera nuance per view.

![ai landscaping design](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/6923c27ca6df8839e5033450_megami2-ezgif.com-gif-to-webp-converter.webp)

For movement, keep it graceful: slow zooms, subtle wind, a passing bird for scale. Export plant schedules and cost estimates to keep clients and contractors aligned—****visual and quantitative storytelling in one workflow****.
