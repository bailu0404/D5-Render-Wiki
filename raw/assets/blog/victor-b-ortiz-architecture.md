## ****Commitment to Green Design****

Eco-friendliness and sustainability lie at the core of Victor's practices. You could see that many of his design perfectly connect nature and construction together, making the whole space full of liveliness.

Urupê Tower, for example, adorned with luxuriant vegetation sprouting from tiered terraces, is poised to be a shining example of green architecture amidst Brazil's urban landscape. The Urupê Tower embodies a dedication to ecological balance and progressive design.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/656d752d9b90eacc39e84d85_Group%201%20(2).jpg)

‍

This inspiring plant stair, presented with D5 Render, features an aesthetic layout of lush and realistic vegetation. While testing how to match different kinds of plant, D5's accurate real-time viewport comes in handy.

In this project, audience could marvel at the striking interplay of nature and architecture, as reflections of the sky, clouds, and plants dance on the glass facade. As a real-time rendering tool, D5's capability of precisely showing the reflections assists the studio a lot in both design and output.

## ****Measures Tailored to Local Conditions****

Victor receives projects from all around the world. From the California coast to tropical Africa, Victor always designs architecture that is appropriate for the local environment and customs.

In line with the principle of eco-friendly design, he frequently embellishes buildings with an array of local flora, including shrubs, conifers, and broadleaf trees of diverse species and forms.

Luckily, the D5 Asset Library covering a wide collection of vegetation models from around the globe make landscaping much easier.
