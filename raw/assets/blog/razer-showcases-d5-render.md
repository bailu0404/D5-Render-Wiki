# Razer Worldwide Showcases D5 Render: Innovation in Action

Since late March, NVIDIA has partnered with Razer to showcase cutting-edge real-time 3D visualization powered by [NVIDIA® GeForce RTX™ GPUs](https://www.d5render.com/post/choose-the-right-video-driver-for-d5-render) and DLSS 4 at Razer stores globally. Featuring D5 Render, these demonstrations highlight how AI and GPU technology are transforming architectural workflows, from design development to client presentations.

## ****Experience the Future of Architectural Visualization at Razer Stores****

At Razer stores worldwide, architects and designers can:

- Test Razer Blade laptops powered by RTX GPUs.
- Explore D5 Render’s real-time capabilities tailored for architectural workflows.
- Learn from experts about integrating AI-powered rendering into everyday design practices.

Experience how NVIDIA and Razer are redefining the future of architectural visualization and creative project delivery.

## ****Revolutionizing Architectural Visualization with D5 Render and DLSS 4****

D5 Render empowers architects and designers with:

- Instant scene updates for high-fidelity lighting, materials, and environmental details.
- Up to 35% faster project timelines for quicker client approvals.
- Centralized [material libraries](https://www.d5render.com/assets) to maintain design consistency.

Enhanced by DLSS 4, D5 Render ensures smoother performance, even in large-scale, complex architectural models.
