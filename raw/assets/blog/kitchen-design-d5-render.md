### Step 1: Import Your Kitchen Design from SketchUp

****Getting started with D5 Render is straightforward.****

1. ****Import the Model****  
   Export your kitchen design from SketchUp and load it into D5 Render. D5's Object List helps you organize your geometry and materials effortlessly, and any updates you make in SketchUp are instantly applied in D5 with the reloading feature—no re-importing needed.
2. ****Navigate the Scene****  
   Use D5's navigation tools to explore camera angles and viewpoints. Switching to two-point perspective is ideal for balanced, architectural shots, giving your kitchen render a clean, polished look that clients will love.
3. ****Apply Base Materials****  
   D5's [Asset Library](https://www.d5render.com/assets?utm_source=d5blog&utm_medium=seo&utm_campaign=kitchen-renders) includes everything from polished concrete to textured wood, so you can quickly apply the right finishes.

- ****Using Texture Maps****: Combine diffuse, normal, and roughness maps for an extra layer of detail. For irregular surfaces, try D5's ****tri-planar mapping tool**** to keep textures consistent and seamless, even on curved edges or unique shapes.

### Step 3: Perfecting [Lighting](https://www.d5render.com/posts/best-practices-of-interior-lighting-for-sketchup?utm_source=d5blog&utm_medium=seo&utm_campaign=kitchen-renders) for an Immersive Scene

Lighting is key to creating a believable, inviting space. Here's how to set it up effectively:

- ****HDRI and Volumetric Lighting****  
  Use HDRI lighting for soft, natural tones, then adjust the sun angle to create subtle shadows and depth. Adding volumetric lighting enhances ambiance, especially for kitchens.
- ****Rectangular Lights for Highlights****  
  Position rectangular lights over countertops to highlight textures and create focal points. Adjust the intensity to avoid harsh highlights for a balanced look.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/673aa5e569b307bb9074d833_5eecdaf48460cde52a8c11d3b16cd324db0890f76f0a6fbc58e70b814913bc36fa0941a66823e44fec177c308ebd5304bfee72aefcde9396b0f07a7e45a77a86f5ea01be9e372e4ee3d8f748aa842b2b57119c0822fac94c4fb4c8ed7016461c.gif)
