# Vizualizime Arkitekture: Boost Animation with D5 Render

The studio's visualization workflow is a meticulous process that begins with a robust foundation—a 3ds Max model. From there, the team embarks on a creative journey, infusing the model with rich textures and populating the scene to encapsulate the envisioned environment. Furnishings are thoughtfully placed, ensuring each space is not only aesthetically pleasing but also tells a story.

‍

Camera angles are carefully selected and set, with lighting and special effects meticulously tailored to accentuate the architectural features of each project. The studio prides itself on attention to detail, adding intricate elements to each scene, thereby enhancing the visual narrative and providing clients with a compelling glimpse into the future of their investments.
