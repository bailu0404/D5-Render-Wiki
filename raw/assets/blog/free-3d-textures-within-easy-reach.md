## How to use free 3d textures?

Install D5 Render and the live-sync plugin D5 Converter, which can sync materials from the modeling software to D5, on [the official website](https://www.d5render.com/download).

Then, start D5 Converter to sync your model and materials.

In D5, you will see an "Assets" button on the top left. Click on it and the D5 Asset Library will open. Here you get plenty of free materials to choose from.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/68512f52b14ab4055e6b8361_63a91338a014a550300a7d98_5.png)

Having decided which one to use, select the material, and click on the model to apply the material onto its surface.
