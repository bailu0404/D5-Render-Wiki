# Crafting Visual Masterpieces: Ahmed Abdelgafard's Approach with D5 Render

[Ahmed Abdelgafard](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ahmed-abdelgafar-a76882151/), a landscape architect and educator at the American University in Cairo, brings his passion for design to life using ****D5 Render****. With over a decade of experience, Ahmed's expertise shines through his certifications and accomplishments. He became a ****Certified Autodesk Revit Professional**** in 2015 and completed a ****Virtual Reality course**** at Ziggurat Global Institute of Technology in 2023. His ****AI-Umma Park**** design in Iraq earned recognition from ****Archdaily**** in 2019, and he was ****shortlisted**** in the ****ACUD competition**** for his innovative street landscape design in Cairo's New Capital.

> **"What inspired me to integrate D5 into my curriculum is my own experience as a student. I had a hard time finding the right tools, so when I found D5, I decided to spread the knowledge."**

### What drew you to D5 Render, and why did you incorporate it into your teaching?

I was searching for real-time render engines that could be compared to Enscape, and I found D5 on a list. After watching its launch, I really liked it. The quality stood out to me, and I was impressed with how the D5 team kept up with updates and upgrades.

What inspired me to integrate D5 into my curriculum is my own experience as a student. I had a hard time finding the right tools, so when I found D5, I decided to spread the knowledge. It's part of my job and ethics to keep students updated. D5 also has that "wow" factor that makes it perfect for presentations, something other programs often lack.

## ****Experience with D5 Render****

> **“The first time I used D5, I was surprised by how much it stood out with its quality materials and library. It really changed my workflow.”**

### ****What stood out to you when you first started using D5 Render?****

When I first used D5, I thought the interface looked complicated. But after hearing about updates and competitions, I decided to give it another shot, and it didn't disappoint. The quality of materials, the extensive library, and the ease of file integration really stood out.

### How does D5 Render compare to other software?

They're confused at first, but after the first session, they become enthusiastic and often say, "Why didn't anyone tell us about this earlier? Why are we using Lumion and Enscape?" D5 is fast and easy to use, and it produces realistic renders very quickly. I see a noticeable improvement in their work. Some students who didn't attend class thought their classmates had asked external students for help because the renders looked so professional!

> **"D5 saved my students so much time. In architecture, time is often limited, and D5 helps you produce high-quality renders in a short amount of time."**
