### ****②**** "Advanced Brush": The Fastest Way to Paint Vegetation

[D5 Render 2.11](https://www.d5render.com/posts/d5-render-2-11-ai-tools-fast-workflow-upgrades) introduces a game-changing ****Advanced Brush**** powered by procedural content generation (PCG) technology. This innovative tool allows you to paint vegetation directly onto surfaces of any material or model, instantly transforming blank terrains into lush landscapes with a single stroke—a breakthrough for ****environment design****workflows.

- Zone Division Modes automatically detect edges with adjustable width and blur settings, while brush subdivision enables detailed area refinement
- New Brush & Eraser editing lets you add or remove plants while preserving existing parameter settings
- Brush Exclusion creates custom no-growth zones by simply painting over area
- Isolation Mode (activated via double-click + N shortcut) groups selected plants for easy batch editing

These Smart Planting capabilities combine the speed of procedural generation with artist-level control—perfect for quickly establishing base vegetation before refining details with traditional scatter diagram-based tools.
