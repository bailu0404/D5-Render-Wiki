# D5 Render with NVIDIA, Intel at Computex 2024

The globally renowned technology exposition, Computex, recently concluded its latest session in Taipei, running from June 4 to June 7, 2024. This year, the expo emphasized its theme of "connecting AI," showcasing cutting-edge developments across AI Computing, Advanced Connectivity, Future Mobility, Immersive Reality, Sustainability, and Innovations.

At Computex, industry leaders [NVIDIA](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/rtx-ai-pc-studio-computex/?utm_source=d5blog&utm_medium=seo&utm_campaign=d5-computex-2024) and [Intel](https://www.intel.com/content/www/us/en/homepage.html?utm_source=d5blog&utm_medium=seo&utm_campaign=d5-computex-2024) showcased their collaborative efforts with D5 Render, highlighting the significant enhancements in productivity and creativity this partnership brings to global architecture and design practices.

## NVIDIA X D5 Render

‍  
NVIDIA has integrated its DLSS 3.5 technology with Ray Reconstruction into D5 Render right from the start. This advanced AI neural network is capable of recognizing diverse scenes and generating additional pixels between sampled rays, resulting in a noise-free real-time viewport. This integration eliminates the need of repetitive reviews and revises, enabling a "what you see is what you get" experience.

## Intel x D5 Render

‍  
At Computex, Intel showcased the AI-enhanced capabilities of D5 Render on laptops powered by the Intel Core Ultra 155H processor. Features like AI Atmosphere Match, which enables one-click sky setups, and Text to 3D, which simplifies model generation, are designed to streamline the workflow significantly for professionals in architecture and design.
