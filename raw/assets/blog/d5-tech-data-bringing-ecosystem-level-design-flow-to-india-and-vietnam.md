# D5 × Tech Data: Bringing Ecosystem-Level Design Flow to India and Vietnam

### ****Key Takeaways:****

- D5 Render is expanding its footprint in India and Vietnam through a strengthened partnership with Tech Data to support fast-growing AEC markets.
- The expansion builds on the launch of D5 3.0, reinforcing D5’s vision for unified, real-time design and visualization workflows.
- Tech Data’s distribution platform enables scalable, local access to D5’s AI-powered visualization tools for firms of all sizes.

‍

****D5 Render****, a global leader in real-time design and visualization for the AEC industry, announced the expansion of its presence in ****India and Vietnam**** through an extended strategic partnership with ****Tech Data****, a TD SYNNEX (NYSE: SNX) company.

The expansion follows a ****milestone year for D5 Render****, marked by the launch of [****D5 3.0****](https://www.d5render.com/posts/d5-render-updates/?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=d5-td)—a major platform update that reinforces the company’s long-term vision for real-time, design-integrated visualization workflows. With growing demand for faster iteration, clearer communication, and higher visual fidelity across architectural projects, D5 Render is scaling its global reach to support design teams operating under increasingly compressed timelines.

Through Tech Data’s established reseller ecosystem and digital distribution infrastructure, architects, interior designers, engineers, and visualization professionals in India and Vietnam will gain access to ****D5 Render’s full suite of solutions****, enabling real-time visualization from early design exploration through final presentation.

As construction and urban development accelerate across both markets, D5 Render addresses a critical industry challenge: enabling teams to ****visualize and refine design intent in real time****, without separating design from visualization. Built on high-performance real-time path tracing and AI-driven technologies, D5 Render allows professionals to create photorealistic images and animations more efficiently—supporting faster decision-making and more confident project delivery.

“These markets require innovative tools to keep pace with rapid development. D5 Render’s ability to deliver stunning visuals in real time is a real game-changer for these markets," said ****Jaap Smit, Senior Vice President, Datech & Business Applications Global, Tech Data.**** “Expanding availability in India and Vietnam ensures that local professionals can adopt real-time visualization workflows that keep pace with rapid development and rising expectations for visual quality.”

For D5 Render, the expansion represents a strategic move to extend its ecosystem into high-growth regions where real-time workflows are becoming essential rather than optional.

"We are incredibly excited to deepen our relationship with Tech Data, building on our successful collaborations in India and Vietnam markets," said ****Niu Zeping, CEO of D5.**** "India and Vietnam are dynamic markets where innovation and design excellence are highly valued. As we move into India and Vietnam, Tech Data's established local footprint and deep understanding of the AEC Industry are indispensable. Tech Data’s strong distribution platform and commitment to value-added services make them our ideal partner to introduce the power of D5 Render to local architects, designers, and visualization artists."

Through this partnership, D5 Render will be delivered via Tech Data’s ****Datech Digital Distribution Platform****, providing streamlined access to licenses, subscription management, and essential business resources. The platform supports scalable adoption across individual practitioners, studios, and enterprise teams, while enabling efficient software lifecycle management through Tech Data’s established reseller network.

This expansion underscores D5 Render’s continued investment in global accessibility—bringing real-time, AI-powered visualization workflows to design professionals in regions shaping the next generation of built environments.
