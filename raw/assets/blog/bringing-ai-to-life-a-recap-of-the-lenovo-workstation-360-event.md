# Bringing AI to Life: Lenovo AI Workstation Event Recap

On August 15, 2024, we had the privilege of joining industry leaders at the Lenovo Workstation 360 event at Guoco Midtown. This was no ordinary tech showcase—it was a deep dive into the future of design and technology, particularly how AI is transforming the way we approach architecture, engineering, and construction (AEC).

### ****The Power of AI in D5 Render****

One of the highlights was the live demo of D5 Render’s AI features. If you’ve ever found yourself bogged down by the tedious details of texture mapping or lighting adjustments, this demo was a revelation. D5 Render’s AI tools make these tasks almost effortless.

- ****AI-generated Material Texture Maps****: Imagine taking a single material and letting AI create multiple realistic variations in seconds. That’s what D5 Render can do, saving you hours of manual work.  
  ‍
- ****AI Atmosphere Match****: Need to match your 3D scene’s mood to a particular image? The AI Atmosphere Match does this automatically, adjusting the lighting and environmental settings to get the look just right

![lenovo ai workstation](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/66c3d001497452f174dda024_66c3cc370d1fe52f4ff4410b_AIatmosphere%2520(1).gif)

- ****Ultra HD Texture****: We also saw how AI can take a basic, low-res texture and upscale it to 4K quality. This is a game-changer for anyone who cares about the fine details in their renderings.  
  ‍
- ****Make Seamless****: No more visible seams when applying textures to models—D5 Render’s AI can smooth these out, making everything look naturally integrated.
