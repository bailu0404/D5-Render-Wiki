# 220+ new animated plant models in D5 Asset Library

[Embedded video](https://www.youtube.com/embed/b7KHOnf-NFI)

The 220+ new animated vegetation models in the [D5 Render](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=animated-plants)'s Asset Library are designed to meet the needs of different landscape projects, ranging from small-scale residential gardens to large public spaces and urban developments.

When paired with animation, these animated plant models will take your projects up a notch.

**You can find all of them by searching “new spring plants” in the library.**
