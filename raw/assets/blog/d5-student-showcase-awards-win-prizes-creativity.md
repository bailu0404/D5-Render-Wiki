## ‍****✅ Evaluation Criteria****

We strongly encourage students to go beyond standard renderings by using [D5](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=d5-student-showcase-award-win-prizes-creativity) to produce a diverse set of drawings that together tell a complete design story. And we’ve broken down our judging criteria into three key categories:

### 1️⃣ ****Diversity of Drawing Types & Completeness of Expression (30%)****

- Use of diagrams, floor plans, sections, elevations, axonometric views, exploded views, and more
- Integration of ****multiple drawing types**** into a complete and coherent design narrative
- Break away from the conventional notion that [D5](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=d5-student-showcase-award-win-prizes-creativity) is only for renderings — showcase how it can support comprehensive architectural communication

‍

### 2️⃣ ****Visual Quality & Presentation Aesthetics (40%)****

- Effective composition and layout with a sense of balance and hierarchy
- Sophisticated use of lighting, materials, colors, and shadows
- Technically refined and visually polished presentation

‍

### 3️⃣ ****Innovation & Creative Use of Tools (30%)****

- ‍[****Creative use of D5 tools****](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=d5-student-showcase-award-win-prizes-creativity) such as lighting effects, animations, or built-in AI features
- Presentation of original spatial concepts or storytelling formats
- Integration of motion, non-traditional media, or other expressive methods
- A distinct and personal approach that pushes the boundaries of conventional design communication

## ‍****💬 FAQ****

### ****Q: Who is eligible?****

****A:**** Students currently enrolled in architecture, design, landscape, or urban planning programs (undergraduate, master’s, or doctoral) and registered as D5 EDU users.

### ****Q: How many times can I submit?****

****A:**** Each participant may submit once per quarter. If your work is not selected, you’re welcome to resubmit in the next quarter.

### ****Q: Can I submit if I don’t use a D5 EDU account?****

****A:**** Sorry, all submissions must be made with a D5 EDU account. Please apply for the Education license first to qualify.

### ****Q: Can I submit a team project? How should I indicate that?****

****A:**** Yes, team submissions are allowed. Please clearly specify which parts you were responsible for and confirm all team members agree to the submission.

### ****Q: How can I check the status or results of my submission?****

****A:**** Results will be announced on the specified quarterly dates. Please stay tuned to official emails and website updates.
