# Toy Arch 2024 Winners: Stunning Designs with D5 Render

The TOY ARCH (Thesis of the Year Awards) in Thailand celebrates architectural excellence among students. Organized by the Council of Deans of Architecture Schools of Thailand (CDAST) and supported by the Association of Siamese Architects, the competition encourages innovative thinking and outstanding design skills. The 2024 competition honored Athasan Poolpipat and Sorlahuddin Chuaykarn, who credited D5 Render as a key tool in achieving their award-winning results.

To read this article in Thai, please click [here](https://www.d5render.com/posts/toy-arch-winner-use-d5-render).

Athasan’s thesis focuses on revitalizing Khao Lak, a surf town in Thailand, by blending surf culture with the local traditions of the Paranagan community and the Mogan tribes. His designs emphasize creativity, freedom, and cultural integration, which D5 Render helped bring to life with stunning realism.
