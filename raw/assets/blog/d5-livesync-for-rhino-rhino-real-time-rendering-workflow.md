Recently, the real-time rendering software, D5 Render, released a demo video of its livesync plugin for Rhino. This video provides a detailed introduction to the seamless integration between D5 and Rhino.

The real-time rendering capabilities of D5 offer Rhino users a detailed and realistic preview that aids in making informed choices regarding lighting, material, vegetation, and other design elements.

## Livesync Capabilities

The livesync plugin seamlessly syncs updates made to Rhino's models, materials, perspectives, and lights directly with D5. This eliminates the need for manual file exports or imports, allowing designers to remain deeply engaged in the creative process.

## Realistic Preview of Design in Rhino

Beyond the convenience of livesync, Rhino designers can now scrutinize their design choices under the realistic lighting conditions provided by D5's real-time raytracing viewport.

This feature elevates the authenticity of the design phase, enabling architects to create more precise depictions of their projects before reaching the final visualization stage. For Rhino architects and landscape designers, D5 Render offers unique benefits such as:

- Environmental presets and AI-assisted environment configuration
- A broad selection of essential architectural materials like glass, wood, marble, and concrete
- The D5 Scatter procedural landscape tool, which allows for precise placement of plant arrangements in specific areas
- An extensive vegetation model library that includes thousands of plant models from across the globe.
