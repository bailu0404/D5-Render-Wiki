## Early Challenges

Prior to using [D5 Render](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=affordable-architectural-kpfs-innovation-leap-with-d5-render-boosting-efficiency-and-cutting-costs), KPF faced several challenges in their design visualization process due to the limitations of established 3D software:

1. Limited features and ****AI integration****
2. Poor quality of ****photorealistic render****
3. Not scalable with ****large-scale, high-profile projects****

Adopting new software also presented challenges:

- Ensuring seamless integration with the existing ****Revit and Rhino workflows****‍
- Minimizing the ****learning curve**** for the team

Resolving these hurdles was crucial to empowering their architects and elevating their workflows, so KPF knew that finding the right solution could be a transformative step forward.

## Discovering D5 Render

Their search led them to [D5 Render](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=affordable-architectural-kpfs-innovation-leap-with-d5-render-boosting-efficiency-and-cutting-costs), a real-time rendering engine that promised to redefine their approach to design visualization.

## Results

### ****Time****

As KPF often handles large-scale scenes requiring urban context blocks or natural environments, [****D5 Scatter****](https://youtu.be/8W7qGRRBNT4?si=My7hh2_53-zw3gXE) addresses this need by providing the function of quickly populating areas with high-quality vegetation assets and saving hours of manual work. Assets like ****interior parallax**** models, which simulate detailed interiors with one click, help to significantly reduce tedious tasks. ****Camera movement templates****, ****multi-editor editing****, and other features in D5 Render, along with its industry-leading real-time rendering algorithms, all contribute to the reduced rendering time.

With D5's AI Atmosphere Match feature, tasks that used to take a week to produce multiple client drafts can now be completed in an afternoon, cutting iteration time by up to ****80%****. This enhancement has significantly boosted communication efficiency both internally and externally.

‍

### ****Cost****

Improved project turnaround times allowed for better allocation of team resources and increased project capacity. Reducing reliance on outsourcing for renderings also saved significant time and cost, typically in the ****tens of thousands of dollars per project****.

The use of D5, along with its license upgrades, has removed obstacles for new users, minimizing the costs associated with learning and adaptation.
