# DSN Intervention Case: D5 Render's Efficiency Breakthrough

In the F&B and hospitality industries, having creative ideas isn't enough to win over clients. To truly stand out, designers need to present visuals that vividly bring their concepts to life—helping clients see and feel the full potential of the design from the very first impression.

For [DSN Intervention](https://www.dsnintervention.com/), an innovative design-and-build firm in Indonesia specializing in F&B interior design, this meant redefining their approach. With over a decade of experience, they embraced real-time architectural rendering to streamlined their workflow, fostered stronger collaboration, and unleashed new creative potential.

## ****Key Takeaways****

1. ****In-House Rendering is a Game-Changer****  
   It gives firms control over timelines, reduces costs, and improves client communication.
2. ****Choose the Right Tools****  
   Opt for software with intuitive workflows, seamless integration, and real-time updates.
3. ****Client-Centric Design****  
   Real-time rendering fosters collaboration, enabling clients to visualize and influence designs from the outset.

- ****Location:**** Indonesia
- ****Team Size:**** 10

## ****From Outsourcing to In-House: A Bold Shift****

For years, DSN Intervention outsourced their 3D rendering needs. Models created in ****SketchUp**** were sent to external renderers using tools like [****3ds Max****](https://www.d5render.com/workflow/3ds-max?utm_source=d5blog&utm_medium=seo&utm_campaign=dsn-indonesia) and ****Corona Renderer****. While this approach let them focus on design, it came with significant challenges:

- ****Lengthy Timelines****: Outsourced renders often took 1–2 weeks, delaying projects.
- ****Limited Revisions****: Communication barriers made quick iterations difficult.
- ****Misaligned Results****: Clients struggled to interpret SketchUp views, leading to misunderstandings and extra revisions.

To overcome these roadblocks, ****Ivan Wirasa Ersan****, DSN Intervention Principal, decided to bring rendering in-house. This decision not only addressed their pain points but also opened the door to faster, more flexible workflows.

## ****Why D5 Render Became the Ultimate Solution****

After exploring tools like ****Twinmotion**** and ****Lumion****, Ivan and his team discovered ****D5 Render**** in late 2022—and it checked every box.

#### ****1. Easy to Use****

D5's intuitive interface made it easy for the team to get started. Features like [****AI Atmosphere Match****](https://www.d5render.com/posts/rain-moment-street-ai-architecture-d5-workflow?utm_source=d5blog&utm_medium=seo&utm_campaign=dsn-indonesia) streamlined realistic lighting setup, giving them more time to refine designs.

#### ****2. Unmatched Speed****

Real-time rendering slashed timelines dramatically. High-quality 4K renders, which used to take 1–2 weeks, could now be completed in ****under 2 minutes****. This speed allowed for immediate client feedback and rapid iterations.

## ****Workflow Transformation with Real-Time Rendering****

> **"Previously, rendering was a bottleneck in our workflow. Now, we can complete five projects in two weeks—a pace unimaginable with our old system. Our clients love seeing their ideas come to life in real-time, which has cut approval times by nearly half."**

Adopting D5 Render didn't just improve efficiency—it completely redefined how DSN Intervention approached interior design.

- ****Increased Productivity****: A single designer can now handle an entire project, enabling the team to complete up to five projects in two weeks—far exceeding their previous output.
- ****Streamlined Revisions****: Real-time updates allowed the team to perfect designs internally before presenting them to clients, leading to smoother communication and better results.
- ****Creative Freedom****: Faster renders freed the team to experiment with bold ideas. **“We always start with bold concepts and refine them later. With D5, revisions are seamless, so creativity flows naturally,”** Ivan shared.
