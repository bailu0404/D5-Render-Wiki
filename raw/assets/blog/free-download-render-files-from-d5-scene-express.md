## ****How to Download Scene Files?****

To download the scene you desire, simply click into the post to access the download link. Each post has details about the artist, the content, and the preview.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/665d5f31e29831a4b6396c30_image%20(3).avif)

‍

Unzip the downloaded package, double-click on the .drs file, and you'll be able to open the scene using [D5 Render](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=d5-render-download) (please make sure to download and install D5 Render before opening the file).
