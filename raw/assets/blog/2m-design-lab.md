# 2M Design Lab: Smooth In-house Collaboration with D5 Render

[2M Design Lab](https://2mdesignlab.com/), based in Bali, is an international architecture studio led by Manuele Mossoni, known for its innovative approach to tropical design. The studio leverages advanced technologies such as Virtual Reality (VR) to address challenges posed by climate change and construction complexities.

‍

2M Design Lab has been leveraging D5 Render across a diverse range of property projects—including residential, hospitality, commercial, and master plan developments—for the past two years. As a studio that encompasses both design and visualization teams, 2M Design Lab will discuss how they have optimized their daily workflows using D5 Render.

## ****Streamlined Exterior & Interior Design****

‍

2M Design Lab specializes in a diverse range of design disciplines including architecture, landscape, and interior, with many of their projects thoughtfully integrated into mountainous landscapes, embracing the natural contours of the terrain.

Thus, Revit is the preferred software for 2M Design Lab's exterior designers due to its precise modeling capabilities, which include detailed geographic and spatial data. However, for designing interior spaces that often feature irregularly shaped decorations, SketchUp proves to be a more time-efficient option.

## ****Seamless Workflow between Designers and Visualization Specialists****

‍

To enhance decision-making and refine the design process, architects seek to evaluate the structure and the site within a realistic setting. To facilitate this, 2M Design Lab has effectively integrated design and visualization through the innovative use of D5 Render.

- ****Quick Experiments with Sky and Atmosphere Using AI****

The AI Atmosphere Match feature of D5 Render significantly enhances the architectural design process by allowing architects to effortlessly simulate various lighting conditions or weather scenarios. By simply uploading reference images into D5, the feature integrates them with the desired view, instantly generating the corresponding sky and post-production effects.

This automation eliminates the tedious task of manually adjusting settings during the initial conceptual phase, thereby saving considerable time.

## ****Enhanced Project Presentations for Clients****

‍

2M Design Lab has always been digging new technologies to present their designs to clients interactively and immersively. Working with D5 Render, the studio can get realistic outputs in a short time.

- ****Image****

D5 Render combines real-time ray tracing, an extensive asset library, and GPU acceleration to produce realistic images quickly. Its accurate global illumination and high-quality assets enhance scene realism, making it ideal for efficient and convincing architectural visualizations.
