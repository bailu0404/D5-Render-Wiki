# Master Your Design Resources with D5 Studio

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/66e955261e8b59fd9d784223_66e953a0b4394b2079561f8b_be-maarch.jpeg)

‍

Keeping your design resources organized is key to a smooth and productive workflow. A well-structured process not only boosts efficiency but also sparks creativity. That's exactly what D5 Studio, a cloud-based platform by D5 Render, aims to achieve. It's packed with features to help teams manage their resources effortlessly while keeping their creative juices flowing. Let's explore the standout features of D5 Studio that make it an essential tool for design teams.

## ****Feature Highlights of D5 Studio****

### ****Effect Presets****

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/66e952356a70e41b56c1ac39_image.jpeg)

### ‍

Effect presets are one of the standout features in D5 Studio. They let you save and reuse settings for environment effects, brush adjustments, and path parameters. Storing these presets in the cloud means you can quickly apply them to new projects, saving time and keeping your designs consistent and high-quality.

### ****Brush Presets****

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/66e9511fdd713f7e4a5f396c_image.jpeg)

Another handy tool is the [brush presets](https://docs.d5render.com/user-guide/tool/how-to-save-and-reuse-presets-for-the-brush-tool?utm_source=d5blog&utm_medium=seo&utm_campaign=resources-d5-studio). These are designed to store asset IDs and brush parameters, making it easy to access your most-used settings without having to set them up each time. This is a real time-saver, especially for teams that need to maintain a consistent look and feel across multiple projects.

### ****Path Presets****

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/66e951be7aa9d3456963135a_image.jpeg)

### ‍

[Path presets](https://docs.d5render.com/user-guide/tool/how-to-save-and-reuse-presets-for-the-path-tool?utm_source=d5blog&utm_medium=seo&utm_campaign=resources-d5-studio) are all about making path creation easier. They allow you to save the parameters you use most often, so you can whip up complex paths quickly and accurately, keeping your projects moving forward without unnecessary delays.

## ****Advanced Resource Management****

D5 Studio takes managing resources to a whole new level. Need to share a preset or an entire folder with your team? No problem. Just copy the data to the designated team folder. The platform also includes role-based permissions, meaning only administrators and owners can manage presets in Team Space. This keeps things organized and secure.

### ****Leveraging D5 Curated for Design Inspiration****

A great feature within D5 Studio is [****D5 Curated****](https://docs.d5render.com/learn-more/how-to-use-d5-studio/share-presets-with-team?utm_source=d5blog&utm_medium=seo&utm_campaign=resources-d5-studio). This section is a goldmine of presets from top designers and design teams, updated regularly to provide fresh ideas and high-quality resources. It's perfect for sparking creativity and finding inspiration for your next big project.

### ****Managing Cloud Storage Efficiently****

D5 Studio offers 100GB of shared cloud storage, giving your team plenty of space to store all the important stuff. You can easily keep track of your storage usage through the Team Settings and Dashboard. Need more room? Just delete outdated files or reach out to support to request additional space.

## ****Conclusion****

D5 Studio is all about helping design teams stay organized, inspired, and efficient. From effect presets and brush settings to managing cloud storage and finding new ideas with D5 Curated, it has everything you need to keep your workflow smooth and your creativity flowing. By making the most of these features, your team can cut down on busy work and focus on what really matters—creating stunning designs.
