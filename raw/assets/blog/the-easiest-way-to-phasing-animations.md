- ****Product Demonstrations****: Highlight step-by-step processes or features.
- ****Structural Development****: Showcase construction stages or material layering.

## ****Getting Started: Preparing for Phasing Animation****

Creating a seamless animation begins with proper preparation. For this, you'll need two essential tools:

1. ****SketchUp**** (or another supported modeling platform).
2. [****D5 Render SketchUp****](https://www.d5render.com/workflow/sketchup?utm_source=d5blog&utm_medium=seo&utm_campaign=phasing-animation) ****Plugin****, which connects your modeling software to D5 Render.

To start, download the SketchUp plugin from the official D5 Render website. Before moving forward, make sure your models are well-organized into ****groups**** or ****components****. This structure is crucial for smooth functionality during export and import processes.

‍

## ****Step 1: Exporting Models from DCC Software****

The first step in creating phasing animations is exporting your models from the digital content creation (DCC) software using the ****D5 Live Sync plugin****. Here's how to do it, tailored for each supported platform.

‍

### ****For SketchUp****

‍

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/67596fad3aef259961dbb341_67596b0da6c3f9d5bfd54735_Phasing%2520Animation%2520blog%25201%25202.gif)

- Organize your models into separate ****groups**** (e.g., chairs, stools).
- Select these groups and click the ****Group Export**** button, ensuring the structure depth is set to two layers.
- Once exported, your model will retain its organized structure when imported into D5.

‍

### ****For Rhino****

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/67596fad3aef259961dbb2e1_67596d5e9b2a4f464a174de3_Phasing%2520Animation%2520blog%25202%25201.gif)

- Categorize model components into distinct ****layers****.
- Choose an export method:
  - ****By Object Selection**** for specific elements.
  - ****By Layer Selection**** for entire categories.
- Set the retained structure depth and export. The model will then appear in D5 Render organized by Rhino layers.

‍

### ****For 3ds Max****

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/67596fac3aef259961dbb2db_67596e3c110075f6c43f032f_Phasing%2520Animation%2520blog%25203%25201.gif)

- Similar to SketchUp, group related elements (e.g., tiles, pendant lights).
- Use the ****Export**** button, selecting "Selected Only," and set the hierarchy depth to two layers.
- Your structured model will seamlessly transfer into D5.

‍

### ****For Revit****

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/67596fae3aef259961dbb3db_67596eb37b55ac2d21b02f07_Phasing%2520Animation%2520blog%25204%25201.gif)

- Select objects individually or use options like ****Export Selected Family Types**** or ****Export Selected Categories****.
- Import the neatly categorized model into D5 Render.

With the export complete, the next step is importing your models into D5 Render and breathing life into them with animations.

‍

## ****Step 2: Importing and Setting Up in D5 Render****

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/675968a4aaa47c958ffbcbf3_5eecdaf48460cde57a33c9d0a981bf8a9c2f01c2e3ed827658e70b814913bc36fa0941a66823e44fec177c308ebd53042380ab6085122a4360b1de5412b70ee45e6e0022e9d1eb538ee9500415380345aad3ac7ab8f47dd44fb4c8ed7016461c.gif)

Once your models are ready, bring them into D5 Render to set the stage for your animation.

1. ****Import Models****: Open a blank D5 worksheet and import your exported models.
2. ****Align Coordinates****: Use the sync function to align all site and building components.
3. ****Enhance Visuals****: Quickly apply textures and materials to your models. Add landscape features for outdoor spaces to create a richer, more compelling scene.

With the groundwork set, you're ready to explore the animation template panel.

‍

## ****Step 3: Creating the Animation****

Phasing animation in D5 is as intuitive as it is powerful. Follow these steps to create your animation:

1. ****Access the Template Panel****: Click the video button in the top-right corner to access animation templates. Preview each effect through dynamic thumbnails.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/675968a30e194e8a345a2b89_5eecdaf48460cde57a33c9d0a981bf8a9c2f01c2e3ed827658e70b814913bc36fa0941a66823e44fec177c308ebd530475e9ad4474ab384009162bf797f6ed8052a8824b0b428cd076b5e0197886251b33b822794fcd01454fb4c8ed7016461c.gif)

2. ****Select Objects****: Choose models manually or use batch selection for efficiency. Grouping objects simplifies this process and keeps everything organized.
3. ****Adjust Timelines****: Extend or shorten the animation duration as needed, or input a precise value. Rename, duplicate, or relocate animation segments to fine-tune your timeline.

‍

## ****Step 4: Adding Sequence and Effects****

Bring an extra layer of polish to your animation by carefully managing sequences and effects.

### ****Sequence Management****

- Reorder objects easily by dragging and dropping them in the object panel.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/675968a471a265be7cb2080b_5eecdaf48460cde57a33c9d0a981bf8a9c2f01c2e3ed827658e70b814913bc36fa0941a66823e44fec177c308ebd530456a6f13402d84e39901b8feddc945834851827f65fbd09f61da3dada2ba1e53475e617af7e2f14ef4fb4c8ed7016461c.gif)

- For larger projects, use the ****Auto Sort**** feature to arrange elements based on world coordinates or axes.

### ****Time Interval Effects****

Choose from three unique effects:

- ****Simultaneous****: All objects appear at once.
- ****Cascading****: Objects flow smoothly in sequence.
- ****Sequential****: Objects appear one after another for a step-by-step effect.

Real-time previews let you adjust time intervals and perfect your animation.
