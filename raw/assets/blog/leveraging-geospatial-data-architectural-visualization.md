If you're working with a slower internet connection, don't worry! You can easily adjust the 'Maximum Screen Space Error' setting to 100 for a lower-resolution import, which will reduce bandwidth usage without sacrificing too much quality in Cesium within [D5 Render](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=leveraging-geospatial-data-architectural-visualization).
