## ****🛠️ Step-by-Step: Automating Your Scene in 4 Stages****

Let's walk through the professional workflow of building a scene from scratch.

### ****Stage 1: Initiating the Vision****

Start by clicking the [****AI Agent****](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=d5-render-ai-scene-generator-archviz) icon next to the Asset Library and select ****AI Scene Match****. You can choose from three curated D5 prompt presets or input your own description. The AI Agent then captures your current viewport to generate a high-quality reference image.

![Automate 3D scene setup with D5 Render AI scene generator for fast Archviz](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/69859a40f8801b159a1d5b37_06.png)

‍

### ****Stage 2: Realizing the Ambience****

Iterate through the chat as needed. Ask the AI to "make the sunset warmer" or "add more morning mist." Once satisfied with the preview, click Apply Atmosphere. [D5 Render](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=d5-render-ai-scene-generator-archviz) will automatically replicate that specific mood within your 3D scene.

‍

### ****Stage 3: Intelligent Asset Curation****

Once the lighting is established, click Recommend Assets. The AI analyzes your selected reference to suggest relevant vegetation, characters, and scatter presets.

You can also upload site photos for targeted analysis or describe specific needs, such as **"**Please recommend tree varieties suitable for aerial-view projects**,"** and the AI will suggest the perfect models for instant application.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/69859a4c1df7a7dc3a851df0_07.png)

‍

### ****Stage 4: Semantic Light Polish****

Finally, use natural language to let the [D5 AI Agent](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=d5-render-ai-scene-generator-archviz) help you fine-tune lighting and environmental parameters. For example, you can simply say, "increase the brightness and shift the color temperature to a warmer tone." The AI Agent will then apply those changes consistently to all active lights in your scene.

If you want to adjust a specific light instead, click the arrow icon at the bottom-left of the chat box, select the light directly in your viewport, and then start chatting with the AI Agent. From that point on, all instructions will apply exclusively to the selected object, ensuring your focal points are perfect.
