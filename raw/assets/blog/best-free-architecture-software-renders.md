### Step 1: ****Conceptual Design & 2D Drafting****

****1.**** [****LibreCAD****](https://librecad.org/)

A lightweight, open-source 2D CAD platform designed for precision technical drafting, offering DWG compatibility and a minimalist interface reminiscent of AutoCAD.

✅ ******Pros******: IFamiliar AutoCAD-like interface, cross-platform support, and DWG compatibility.

❌ ******Cons******: No 3D capabilities or parametric design features.

******Best for******: Architects needing a no-frills 2D drafting tool for floor plans or schematics.

‍

****2.**** [****FreeCAD****](https://www.freecad.org/) ****(2D Module)****

A parametric modeling powerhouse with dedicated 2D sketching tools, enabling dimension-driven designs and basic BIM workflows.

✅ ******Pros******: Open-source, supports BIM workflows, and customizable with Python scripting.

❌ ******Cons******: Steep learning curve; interface feels clunky for quick ideation.

******Best for******: Technically minded users seeking a free alternative to commercial CAD/BIM software.
