# Winners Revealed: D5 Render Challenge II Global Contest

[Embedded video](https://www.youtube.com/embed/HiMSseR0oL8)

**4 months ago,** [**D5 Render**](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=global-d5-rendering-contest-winners) **announced its second global contest** [******D5 Render Challenge II******](https://forum.d5render.com/t/announcement-d5-render-challenge-ii-distance-how-far-would-you-go/8116) **and was later open for submissions.  
With the aim to free the people from lockdown and other limitations,** [**D5**](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=global-d5-rendering-contest-winners) **held this contest to serve as a tool for its users' imagination and creative ideas. It also offers prize pool of over 19,000 USD including** ******cash prize and D5 Pro license****** **to the winners to reward their efforts and amazing performance.  
Up till now, there have been** ******hundreds of entries****** **from all around the world. The artists expressed their feelings, shared their stories, and visualized their thoughts through rendering in D5.  
Now let's take a look at some of the winning works.**
