# How to Make Snow Effects in D5 Render? (Step-by-Step)

In landscape design, snowscapes often bring special romance, as it can invoke many nice elements in people’s mind, like purity and holiday. But building a realistic snowscape is not that romantic, because it posts challenges for both the designer and the rendering software. Today, we shall share with you some tricks on making a good snowscape with [D5 Render](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=snow-render).

![Snowscape rendered with D5](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/688c947bdb0b03b9dbc1056a_637b0ec603cd698432077d8d_1.jpeg)

[Download the scene for free](https://forum.d5render.com/t/scene-express-vol-173-free-d5-render-scene-snow-mountain/12770)

This model of snow ground, imported from Bridge, already has many details on its surface. You can see it has a natural contrast between light and shadow as well. To not break its original beauty, we have to think twice before adding other decorative assets, snow-covered trees or cabins for example, to match with the snowscape.

Many would directly switch on "Snow" effect in the Environment system after importing cabins or trees. Yet this is not an ideal solution as it would cover the original details of our delicate snow ground model. And the roof of cabin has a very humble snow effect in this way.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/688c947adb0b03b9dbc1054f_637b0ec5359e1d0561a128c4_2.png)

![Snow decals from D5 Asset Library](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/688c947adb0b03b9dbc10552_637b0ec5e055f0e08fd2a0bd_3.png)

Besides, this method will make snow settle evenly on the surface of trees and mountains, which is impossible in the real world due to the influence of gravity, wind, sunlight and many other factors.

So the real answer is to use the snow decals in D5 Asset Library instead. They are like stickers that can be attached to a model's surface.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/688c9479db0b03b9dbc1052e_637b0ec469bf6434723e628d_4.jpeg)

Drag and drop the two free snow decals onto the cabin's roof. Adjust their size and position as you need. Repeat this on the trees and mountains, which will make them have a look that snow on the adret side has melted.

![Snow decals from D5 Asset Library](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/688c947adb0b03b9dbc10543_637b0ec48c7bd882d3f32fae_5.jpeg)

![Snow decals from D5 Asset Library](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/688c947bdb0b03b9dbc1057a_637b0ec5cf7e15e5f800cd8f_6.png)

Everything is done! Now the snowscape looks natural and exquisite.

![Snowscape rendered with D5](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/688c947bdb0b03b9dbc10571_637b12519057b7663ff12211_8.jpeg)
