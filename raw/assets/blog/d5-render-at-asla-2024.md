## Highlights of D5 Render at ASLA 2024

- ****Real-Time Rendering with Seamless Integration****  
  D5 Render integrates smoothly with software like ****Vectorworks**** via the ****D5 Sync plugin****, allowing quick one-click imports and real-time setup of lighting and materials for faster, photorealistic visuals.

- ****Expansive Vegetation & Material Libraries****  
  With over ****3,800 plant models**** and ****1,000+ customizable materials****, D5 Render provides a vast selection for creating detailed, realistic outdoor environments without the need for extra tools or licenses.

- ****Efficient Landscaping Tools****  
  Tools like ****Brush, Path, and PCG-driven Scatter**** enable rapid placement of vegetation, saving time and streamlining the process for large-scale landscape designs.
