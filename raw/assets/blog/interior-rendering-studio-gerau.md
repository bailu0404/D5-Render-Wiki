## ****1. Designing with Purpose: Studio GERAU's Philosophy****

Studio GERAU was founded with a mission to transform spaces intelligently and humanely. For founder Geovana Ribeiro, interior design is about more than aesthetics—it's about aligning space with identity, lifestyle, and wellbeing. The studio specializes in residential interiors, clinics, and consulting rooms, where every detail is thoughtfully considered to create inviting, functional, and emotionally resonant environments.

"A well-designed space has a direct impact on people's quality of life and productivity," Geovana shares. Visualization plays a central role in her process, ensuring ideas are communicated clearly and reducing the need for rework. Over time, the studio's approach has evolved to integrate strategic functionality with highly personalized touches—a hallmark of GERAU's work.
