# 190+New Models for Japanese Designers: Lighting, Ornaments, Furniture, Greenery and More

D5 Asset Library has recently been enhanced with an exciting new collection featuring 198 new Japanese models, including light fixtures, ornaments, furniture, potted plants and greenery, etc. Designers will discover 113 indoor decorations and 85 plant models, all curated by the D5 asset team to perfectly align with your design requests.

To explore more Japanese courtyard plants, check out our previous update: [****170+ New Animated Topiary Models for Oriental Courtyards****](https://forum.d5render.com/t/170-new-animated-topiary-models-for-oriental-courtyards-pine-maple-tallow-tree-and-more/36385).

This collection from the D5 Asset Library features animated models of pines, maples, tallow trees, cherry blossoms, and hackberry trees—specially curated to enhance Japanese garden scenes.

To access these new models quickly, simply search ‘Japanese models’ in the library.
