# KPF & D5 Render Webinar: Master Architectural Visualization

On June 20, 2024, Andy Christoforou and Sharel Liu from KPF teamed up with Jeffery from D5 Render to lead a webinar titled "Shaping the Future: KPF's Innovation Transformation with D5 and AI". Over 2,000 architects, designers, business managers, and D5 users from around the globe had registered to join this event.

![KPF & D5 Render Webinar](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/66a7591f5cdb68e51145edcd_66753bd336c19fe4d62b2ab9_banner.png)

Andy Christoforou is the Project Innovation Manager at KPF, focusing on the exploration and integration of emerging technologies into projects. Sharel, from the Design Technology team, collaborates extensively with Andy to identify and implement optimal technological strategies for architectural design.

![KPF & D5 Render Webinar](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/66a7591f5cdb68e51145edb8_66753bddac5cd21563171fd2_speakers.jpeg)

Attracting 2,000 registrants and over 350 live attendees, the 30-minute session covered the latest trends in computational architectural design and KPF's successful adoption of D5 Render.
