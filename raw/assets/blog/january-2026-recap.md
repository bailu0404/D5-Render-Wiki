# D5 January Recap | A New Chapter Begins

#### ******D5 3.0, D5 Lite, D5 Works, and more — welcome to the new era of real-time design & visualization******

‍

We started 2026 with our biggest launch yet. In January, we introduced a unified creative ecosystem designed to meet you at every stage of the design journey — from early ideation to final delivery. With ****D5 Render 3.0****, ****D5 Lite****, and ****D5 Works****, we’re moving closer to one seamless, AI-powered workflow that keeps creativity in flow.

Here’s a quick look at what went live this month — and how you can make the most of it.

## 1. Product Launches

### ****1.1 D5 Render 3.0****

****D5 3.0 delivers a significant upgrade to real-time visualization workflows**** — combining advanced atmospheric features, AI-assisted tools, and performance enhancements designed for production-ready rendering and animation. Built for high-end visual storytelling, this release is powered by AI to support faster iteration, greater clarity, and uninterrupted creative flow—and integrates seamlessly with D5 Lite for early-stage design.

### ****1.3 D5 Works:**** Curated ****A****ssets for Spatial Design

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/6969b283ec06a93e4a0d027e_5eecdaf48460cde558f7e844b237f77c3d101e187bb2483275b8339e1c4c2483f728f271dbcdaa6639e8703ac5556d0dba1ad177841564a40612da148967d27a238872d1f17506a916151caf5568ed2285b30da42667cf6f0368fdcb069939da.gif)

****D5 Works**** is a 3D asset platform for managing assets, scenes, and workflows—designed to support collaboration across individuals and teams. With curated, render-ready content for spatial design, it helps you store, organize, and share models and presets in one place, fully integrated into your D5 workflow. Whether you're working solo or across studios, D5 Works centralizes everything you need to stay productive.
