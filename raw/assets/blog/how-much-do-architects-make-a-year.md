## 1. How Much Do Architects Make a Year? 2024 Salary Data Revealed

If you're an aspiring architect, a student considering the field, or just curious about the profession, a common question is, "How much do architects make a year?" The straightforward answer is, it varies widely! According to the ****U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics (****[****BLS****](https://data.bls.gov/oesprofile/)****)****, the ****median annual wage for architects**** in ****2024**** is ****$96,510****, with salaries typically ranging from ****$66,130**** for the lowest 10% to over ****$143,780**** for the top 10%. These numbers, however, only tell part of the story.

Experience and location significantly impact earnings for architects. For instance, architects in California often earn well above the national median due to the high demand and cost of living. In contrast, those in states like Florida may earn below the national median. Additionally, entry-level professionals typically start with salaries below the median wage. However, earnings tend to increase with experience and licensure—senior architects or those managing large projects can even exceed the top 10% income bracket.
