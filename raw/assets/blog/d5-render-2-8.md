# D5 Render 2.8 | Advanced AI Enhancer, 22 New Features, and 190+ New Assets!

D5 Render 2.8 has just been released, bringing an array of new features and enhancements meticulously crafted to elevate your rendering projects. This latest version focuses on improving detail, lighting effects, workflow efficiency, and more. Let's explore the comprehensive updates that D5 Render 2.8 offers.

## ****AI Enhancer(beta): Elevating Detail and Precision****

‍

![](http://d5render-web-assets.d5cdn.com/archive/cms/66854046ae946f732216116f_Al%E5%A2%9E%E5%BC%BA1.gif)

‍

The AI Enhancer tool is a standout feature in D5 Render 2.8. It enhances various elements within your renders, such as lighting, materials, characters, vehicles, and vegetation. This tool allows for selective area enhancement with three intensity levels—weak, normal, and strong—ensuring your renders are more detailed and realistic. Additionally, the AI Enhancer Channel improves the accuracy of area selection, giving you precise control over enhancements. You can track and view all enhancement tasks within your D5 account, providing a comprehensive history of your rendering improvements.

‍

Achieve stunning effects like colored gradient glass by enabling the 'Affected by Light' option. Adjusting the opacity value and using black-and-white maps to control the opacity pattern allows for dynamic light interaction.

## ****New and Enhanced Features****

- ****Sculpture and PBR Styles to 'Text to 3D'****: Added new styles to enhance the detail and realism of text-based 3D models.
- ****Optimized AI Make Seamless Interaction****: Uses image analysis technologies to automatically determine and remove seams vertically, horizontally, or in both directions.
- ****Opacity Map to Displacement Material Template****: Enables precise control over the cutting of materials, achieving uneven and hollow effects on hedges, fences, bamboo weaving surfaces, and more.****‍****
- ****Real-Time AO Adjustments:**** Enhance details directly in the viewport with the new "AO Overlay in Preview" feature. Found under Effect > Style, this tool allows real-time adjustment of ambient occlusion (AO) intensity, eliminating the need for post-production software. It streamlines your workflow, reduces export time, and boosts efficiency, enabling designers to fine-tune AO effects on the fly for greater detail and depth.

‍

## ****Performance Improvements****

D5 Render 2.8 includes several optimizations to ensure a smoother rendering experience:

- ****Reduced Memory Usage****: Optimized image rendering algorithms minimize peak memory usage, reducing the risk of crashes.
- ****Enhanced User Experience:**** Designed with the user in mind, D5 Render 2.8 features community-driven language packs, improved gizmo rotation, and mouse speed adjustments in navigation modes, making your design process smoother and more intuitive.

## ****Additional Features****

- ****Upgraded D5 Scatter****: Added Cull Effect to define areas free of scattering plants/objects and supports saving scatter presets for easy reuse.
- ****Visibility Control in Video Mode****: Added visibility toggle for models and layers, facilitating keyframe animations by allowing them to be shown/hidden.
- ****Animation Controller for Models****: Supports animated models in Video mode, enhancing dynamic presentations.
