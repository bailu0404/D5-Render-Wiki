# How to Use Local Library for Ordered Resource Management

For 3D design and visualization, the Local Library in D5 Render simplifies asset management for various projects, enhancing creativity. This blog explores how to use the Local Library effectively, with insights and examples from D5 users.

‍

## ****Customizable Categories for Flexible Workflow****

The Local Library in D5 supports custom resource classification, so you can tailor the menu based on your preference.

- The left sidebar enables you to create, delete, and rename a primary category/subcategory by right-clicking on the name.
- You can create up to three levels of categories for detailed and easy navigation of your library.
- New subcategories appear at the bottom of the list and can be rearranged using drag-and-drop for customized library organization.

You can categorize assets by project, theme, or type in endless ways to meet your specific requirements.

"This system helps me quickly locate and control specific items based on project requirements," said Thierry, a talented architect using D5 Render. He often binds several plants into a group and save it to the local library so that the surrounding landscape in a project could be effectively completed.

## ****Tailored Thumbnails for Quick Recognition****

You can customize thumbnails for each asset to ensure they are easily recognizable, adjusting their size from small to medium or large for optimal visibility.

**"I love the possibility to custom thumbnail for each assets/materials to have a better and stylish preview!"**

‍

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/665d68c91247ca2a06a6b0ae_44.jpg)

A valuable suggestion was offered to enhance efficiency in organizing and swiftly selecting resources by highlighting the significance of crafting clear and precise thumbnails.

‍

**"By adding assets to the local library using a plane base and applying a Pure White HDRI as the background, the thumbnails become much clearer. This method ensures a clear read of the resources in the thumbnails, greatly simplifying the process of searching and selecting assets."**

## ****Batch Management for Greater Efficiency****

To save valuable time, you can click the pencil icon in the upper right corner to manage assets in a batch. After checking the assets, click Move to and choose the target category in the pop-up window. This in-one-go feature is especially useful for quickly organizing large collections of related assets.

‍

**"Batch processing has been a game-changer for me. By organizing and moving multiple assets or materials at once, I save a significant amount of time."**

‍

## ****Search Feature for Precise Narrow-Down****

The Search feature in the Local Library is designed to be consistent with that of the Online Library, providing a seamless experience whether you’re searching through local or online assets. This makes it easier to find specific items, ensuring that you can quickly narrow down your search to locate the assets you need for your project. Use the search bar at the top of the Local Library to enter keywords related to the asset you’re looking for.

‍

**"It allows me to find the assets I need in a matter of seconds, even in large and diverse libraries. I no longer have to waste time scrolling manually."**
