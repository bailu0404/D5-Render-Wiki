## Introducing the Blender-D5 workflow with livesync plugin D5 Sync

D5 Sync is a direct-link plugin that can sync models and lights directly from Blender to [D5 Render](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=d5-render-blender). You can also use it to bake textures with ease and pleasure. D5 Sync-Blender enables you to see the project visualized instantly in D5 while you can model in Blender simultaneously.

- ****D5 Export****  
    
  "Export to .D5A" will export .blend files as .d5a files which can be directly read by D5 Render. The models, materials and lights will all be perfectly brought into [D5](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=d5-render-blender).  
  ‍
- ****D5 Link****  
    
  D5 link enables you to sync models, lights, and later changes to your Blender project to D5 Render in real time.  
  ‍  
  **Export/Sync Models**: send your models and the changes to your models to D5.  
  **Sync Updates**: changes made to the Blender models, materials and meshes can be quickly updated in D5 Render.  
  ‍
- ****D5 Material Bake****  
    
  You can bake all/selected materials with different resolutions. When "using auto-uv" is checked, UV will be automatically mapped.

## Build a realistic block with Blender-D5 real-time rendering workflow

To help you better understand how D5 Render can help you work more efficiently, here we prepare a guide on how to make a realistic block with Blender-D5 real-time rendering workflow.

![d5 render blender](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/6830472acf9d0cb3e20bf97e_638d9276b55f59a64ca2316f_blender-d5%252001.png)

**\*We referred to the Red Hole located in Seoul, South Korea to create the model.**

### 1. Send your Blender project to D5 Render

First things first, check your Blender model and sync the models, lights and baked materials to D5 Render. Subsequent changes made in Blender can also be updated quickly in [D5 Render](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=d5-render-blender).

![d5 render blender](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/6830472aff8737b1d9af9ea6_638d95e214dc6e76965233b4_4.png)

![d5 render blender](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/6830472ada7b4a6b5420ca50_638d95e3f5ce0272d4fa2865_5.png)

To sync models made with curve nodes, you have to convert curve-node models to mesh-node models in Blender in advance.

### 2. Create an atmospheric environment

Here we want a grayish after-rain atmosphere for the street.

The sky needs to be a bit gray, so you can use the ****built-in cloudy HDRI**** or import your own to demonstrate the vibe. Don't forget to adjust the light intensity a bit down.

It's very easy to ****create puddles on the road**** in D5. Just go to Environment > Weather. Turn on Precipitation and adjust the rain Strength to 0. Now the puddles appear.

If you need other weather like fog, snow or clouds, just go to D5 Weather system and you won't be disappointed.

![d5 render blender](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/6830472a70524e4c4b1fac6d_638d95e1ce20b70029d599f2_6.png)

### 3. Edit materials with ease

Material editing is quite simple in [D5 Render](https://www.d5render.com/downloading?utm_source=seo&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=d5-render-blender).  Without complex texture nodes, you just need to drag the texture slider and see how the material is changed in real time. Take this handrail for example.

To make it have a brownish-red metal look, first choose a metal material from the built-in [D5 Asset Library](https://bit.ly/4jxfyrt). Change its color to a dark red tone and adjust "Roughness" and "Metallic" as you like. Turn on Round Corner to create a soft edge. Now the handrail looks realistic and delicate.

![d5 render blender](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/6830472ab9eb100ee91a4b6a_638da57082d52e44ab7997c3_12.png)

![d5 render blender](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/68304729cb49fcd44811668c_638da56e2c86b71fddea8b9e_14.png)

Other materials can be edited in this way as well. D5 Asset Library has ****over**** [****2,000 high-quality materials****](https://bit.ly/4jxfyrt). Drag and drop them into your scene. With a few adjustments to the parameters like UV, Normal, and Roughness, you will have a realistic effect.

![d5 render blender](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/62ce5d829e01c60b7c148396/6830472adf7b69c59673048c_638d96ee1539186dc1566ddc_7.png)
