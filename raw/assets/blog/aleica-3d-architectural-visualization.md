# Driving 3D Architectural Visualization Forward: D5 Render at ALEICA 2025

### Key Takeaways:

- D5 Render strengthens its local presence in Latin America through a partnership with Ironsoft and participation in ALEICA 2025.
- The event showcased how 3D architectural visualization can enhance design communication, decision-making, and storytelling.
- D5 extended its impact through workshops for students and educators, reinforcing its commitment to education and community growth.

D5 Render took center stage at the 6th International Congress of Architecture and Civil Engineering in Guadalajara, Mexico, on May 15–17, 2025, proudly serving as the main sponsor. The event, hosted by the Latin American Association of Civil Engineering and Architecture Students ( ALEICA ), welcomed hundreds of students, professionals, and educators united by a shared passion for shaping the future of design, construction, and urban development.

### Global Technology, Local Connection

In partnership with [Ironsoft](https://ironsoft.online/)—D5’s official reseller in Mexico—and supported by the D5 LATAM team, D5 Render strengthened its local presence with a global touch. This collaboration highlights D5's ongoing commitment to empowering regional communities through trusted local partnerships, ensuring personalized support and deeper industry engagement wherever its users are.
